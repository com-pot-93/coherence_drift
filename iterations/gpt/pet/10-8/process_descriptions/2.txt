The process begins when a deregistration is initiated. After this, the deregistration request is reviewed and verified. Based on this review, there are two possible outcomes:

- If the request is rejected, the process moves to rejecting the deregistration. After the rejection, the process continues to the next step.
- If the request receives preliminary confirmation, the process proceeds to providing preliminary confirmation. After this confirmation, the process continues to the next step.

Regardless of whether the request was rejected or preliminarily confirmed, the process then assigns the GO as the MSP. Following this assignment, the MSPO is informed about the assignment. Next, the MPO is notified of the assignment. After that, the SP is informed of the assignment. The process ends after all these notifications have been completed.
