The process begins with an initial step. First, the files are checked. After this, a decision is made about how to proceed. Depending on the outcome of this decision, one of two actions takes place:

- Either the files are physically tracked,  
- Or a search is initiated for any missing files.

Once either of these actions is completed, another decision point is reached. After this, two activities are carried out at the same time:

- The files are handed to the Associate,
- And the Judge’s Lawlist is distributed to the relevant people.

When both of these activities are finished, the process continues with conducting the directions hearings. After the directions hearings are completed, the process comes to an end.
