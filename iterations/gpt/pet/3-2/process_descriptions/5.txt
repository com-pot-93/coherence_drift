The process begins with an initial step. After this, the files are checked. Based on the outcome of this check, there are two possible paths:

- If the files can be tracked, the process continues with tracking the files.
- If files are missing, a search for the missing files is carried out.

Once either the files have been tracked or the missing files have been found, the process moves forward. At this point, two activities happen at the same time:

- The files are handed to an associate.
- The judge’s lawlist is distributed.

After both of these activities are completed, the process continues with conducting directions hearings. Once the directions hearings are conducted, the process ends.
