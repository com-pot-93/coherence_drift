The process begins when MPOO starts a deregistration request with GO. After receiving this request, GO reviews it.

At this point, GO decides how to proceed. If GO chooses to reject the deregistration request, GO communicates this rejection. Following this, there is another decision point: whether or not to continue with readmission.

If the decision is not to continue with readmission, the choice is recorded, and the process moves towards its conclusion.

If the decision is to continue with readmission, MPOO confirms its intention to proceed. This confirmation is recorded. Next, GO acquires or changes the necessary equipment, then assigns itself as the new MPO. GO then informs MPOO that the previous assignment has ended, notifies MPOO about the new assignment, informs MSPO about the new assignment, and finally informs SP about the new assignment. After these notifications, the process moves towards its conclusion.

Returning to the earlier decision point after GO reviews the deregistration request, if GO does not reject the request, GO provides a preliminary confirmation. Another decision is made: whether or not to continue with readmission.

If the decision is not to continue with readmission, this choice is recorded, and the process moves towards its conclusion.

If the decision is to continue with readmission, MPOO confirms its intention to proceed. This confirmation is recorded. GO then acquires or changes the necessary equipment, assigns itself as the new MPO, informs MPOO that the previous assignment has ended, notifies MPOO about the new assignment, informs MSPO about the new assignment, and finally informs SP about the new assignment. After these notifications, the process moves towards its conclusion.

The process ends after all the necessary steps and notifications have been completed, regardless of which path was taken.
