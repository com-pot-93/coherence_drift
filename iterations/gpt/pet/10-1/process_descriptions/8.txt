The process begins when a dismissal is sent by MPON. After this, MPOO reviews the dismissal. Based on the outcome of this review, there are two possible actions:

- If the decision is to oppose the dismissal, the process continues with the step to oppose the dismissal.
- If the decision is to confirm the dismissal, the process continues with the step to confirm the dismissal.

Regardless of whether the dismissal is opposed or confirmed, the process then moves forward to the next step. After this, the process ends.
