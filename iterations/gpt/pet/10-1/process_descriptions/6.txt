The process begins when a dismissal is sent by MPON. After this, MPOO reviews the dismissal. Based on the review, there are two possible actions: MPOO can either oppose the dismissal or confirm it. If MPOO chooses to oppose the dismissal, they proceed to formally oppose it. If MPOO chooses to confirm the dismissal, they proceed to confirm it. Regardless of whether the dismissal is opposed or confirmed, the process then continues to the next step. Finally, the process ends.
