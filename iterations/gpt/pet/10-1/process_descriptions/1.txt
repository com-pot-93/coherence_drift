The process begins when MPON sends a dismissal. After this, MPOO reviews the dismissal. Based on the review, there are two possible actions:

- If MPOO decides to oppose the dismissal, they take action to oppose it.
- If MPOO decides to confirm the dismissal, they take action to confirm it.

Regardless of whether the dismissal is opposed or confirmed, the process then continues to the next step. After proceeding to the next step, the process ends.
