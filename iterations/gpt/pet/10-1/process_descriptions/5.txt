The process begins when MPON sends a dismissal. After this, MPOO reviews the dismissal. Based on the outcome of this review, there are two possible actions:

- If MPOO decides to oppose the dismissal, they proceed to formally oppose it.
- If MPOO decides to confirm the dismissal, they proceed to confirm it.

Regardless of whether MPOO opposes or confirms the dismissal, the process then continues to the next step. After this, the process ends.
