The process begins when a dismissal is sent by MPON. After this, the dismissal is reviewed by MPOO. Following the review, a decision is made: either to oppose the dismissal or to confirm it.

If the decision is to oppose the dismissal, a formal opposition to the dismissal is made. If the decision is to confirm the dismissal, the dismissal is confirmed.

Regardless of whether the dismissal was formally opposed or confirmed, the process then continues to the next step. After this, the process ends.
