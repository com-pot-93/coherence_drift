The process begins when MPON sends the dismissal. After this, MPOO reviews the dismissal. Based on the outcome of this review, there are two possible actions:

- If MPOO decides to oppose the dismissal, they proceed to formally oppose it.
- If MPOO decides to confirm the dismissal, they proceed to confirm it.

Regardless of whether MPOO opposes or confirms the dismissal, the process continues to the next step, which leads directly to the end of the process.
