The process begins when a claim is received. First, the documents related to the claim are checked. After verifying the documents, the claim is registered. Once the claim is registered, it is picked up for processing.

Next, the insurance details associated with the claim are checked. After this, the validity of the claim is assessed. Based on this assessment, a decision is made:

- If the claim is approved, repairs are authorized. After authorizing repairs, payment is scheduled.
- If the claim is not approved, the claim is rejected.

Regardless of whether the claim is approved or rejected, a decision letter is then sent to inform the claimant of the outcome. The process ends after the decision letter has been sent.
