The process begins when a claim is received. First, the accompanying documents are checked to ensure everything necessary has been provided. Once the documents are verified, the claim is registered in the system.

After registration, the claim is picked up for processing. The next step is to check the insurance details related to the claim. Following this, the validity of the claim is assessed.

At this point, a decision is made based on the assessment. If the claim is approved, repairs are authorized, and then payment is scheduled. If the claim is not approved, the claim is rejected.

Regardless of whether the claim is approved or rejected, a decision letter is sent to inform the claimant of the outcome. The process ends after the decision letter has been sent.
