The process begins when a claim is received. First, the documents related to the claim are checked. After verifying the documents, the claim is registered. Once the claim is registered, it is picked up for processing.

Next, the insurance details are checked. After this, the claim is assessed. Based on the assessment, a decision is made:

- If the claim is approved, repairs are authorized, and then payment is scheduled.
- If the claim is not approved, the claim is rejected.

Regardless of whether the claim is approved or rejected, a decision letter is sent to inform the claimant of the outcome. The process ends after the decision letter is sent.
