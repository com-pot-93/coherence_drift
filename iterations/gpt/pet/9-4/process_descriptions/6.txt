The process begins with the start of the workflow. First, the meeting dates are finalized. Once the dates are set, the group calendars are updated to reflect the scheduled meetings. After updating the calendars, meeting folders are created to organize the necessary materials.

Next, relevant documents are uploaded into these meeting folders. Once the documents are in place, members are advised to read the materials in preparation for the meeting. The meeting is then held as planned.

After the meeting concludes, meeting minutes are produced to record what was discussed and decided. These minutes undergo a quality check to ensure their accuracy and completeness. The results of this quality check are then sent to the appropriate parties.

Following this, departmental records are updated with the finalized meeting minutes and any other relevant information. The process ends after the records have been updated.
