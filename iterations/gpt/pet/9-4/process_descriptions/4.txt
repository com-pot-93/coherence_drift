The process begins with the start of the workflow. First, the meeting dates are finalized. Once the dates are set, the group calendars are updated to reflect the scheduled meetings. After updating the calendars, meeting folders are created to organize all necessary materials.

Next, relevant documents are uploaded into these meeting folders. Once the documents are in place, members are advised to read the uploaded materials in preparation for the meeting. The meeting is then held as planned.

After the meeting concludes, meeting minutes are produced to record what was discussed and decided. These meeting minutes then undergo a quality check to ensure their accuracy and completeness. The results of this quality check are sent out.

Following this, departmental records are updated based on the meeting outcomes and the quality-checked minutes. The process ends after the departmental records have been updated.
