The process begins with the start of the workflow. First, the meeting dates are finalized. Once the dates are set, the group calendars are updated to reflect the scheduled meeting. After updating the calendars, meeting folders are created to organize the necessary materials.

Next, relevant documents are uploaded into these meeting folders. Once the documents are in place, members are advised to read the documents in preparation for the meeting. After members have been notified, the meeting is held as planned.

Following the meeting, a record of the meeting, known as the meeting minutes, is produced. These meeting minutes then undergo a quality check to ensure their accuracy and completeness. The results of this quality check are then sent to the appropriate parties.

After the quality check results have been communicated, the departmental records are updated to include the finalized meeting minutes and any other relevant information. The process concludes at this point.
