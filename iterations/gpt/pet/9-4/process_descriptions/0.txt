The process begins when the Coordination Unit finalizes the dates for the meeting. Once the dates are set, the Support Officer updates all group calendars to reflect the scheduled meeting. After updating the calendars, the Support Officer creates folders for the meeting.

Next, the Support Officer ensures that all the necessary documents are uploaded to the system. Once the documents are available, the Support Officer advises all Committee Members to read these documents in preparation for the meeting.

Following this, the Committee Members hold the meeting as planned. After the meeting concludes, the Support Office produces the minutes of the meeting. The Coordination Unit then conducts a quality check on these minutes to ensure their accuracy.

Once the quality check is complete, the Coordination Unit sends the results of this check to all Committee Members. Finally, the Support Officer updates the departmental records to reflect the outcomes of the meeting. The process ends at this point.
