The process begins when the need to fill a position is identified. Once this need is recognized, a request to fill the position is submitted. After the request has been submitted, the position is advertised to attract potential candidates. Following the advertisement, applications for the position are received. These applications are then reviewed to assess the suitability of the candidates. Based on this review, a suitable candidate is selected. After selecting the candidate, the position is filled. The process concludes once the position has been successfully filled.
