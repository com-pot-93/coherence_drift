The process begins when a need to fill a position is identified. Once this need is recognized, a request is made to have the position filled. After the request is submitted, the position is advertised to attract potential candidates. Following the advertisement, applications from interested individuals are received. These applications are then reviewed to assess the suitability of the applicants. Based on this review, a suitable candidate is selected. Once the selection is made, the position is filled with the chosen candidate. The process concludes after the position has been successfully filled.
