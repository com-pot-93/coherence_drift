The process begins when a need to fill a position is identified. First, the relevant department requests that the position be filled. After this request is made, the position is advertised so that potential candidates are informed about the vacancy. Once the advertisement is published, applicants submit their applications for the position. These applications are then reviewed to ensure they meet the necessary requirements. After the applications have been checked, the position is filled by selecting a suitable candidate. The process concludes once the position has been successfully filled.
