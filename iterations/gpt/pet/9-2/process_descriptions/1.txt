The process begins when a mortgage offer is sent. After sending the offer, the process waits for a reply from the customer.

Once the customer’s reply is received, a decision is made. If the process follows the first path, the case details are updated. After updating the case details, the work is archived, and then a cancellation is performed. The process then moves to a point where it waits for the next step.

Alternatively, the process may move the case to administration. At this stage, another decision is made. If a request for outstanding documents is needed, a request is generated for these documents. After generating the request, the case details are updated, the work is archived, and a cancellation is performed. The process then moves to the next step.

If no request for outstanding documents is needed, the process skips directly to the next step.

Finally, after either path is completed, the process ends.
