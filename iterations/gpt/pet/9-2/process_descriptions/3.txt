The process begins when a mortgage offer is sent. After sending the offer, the process waits for a reply from the customer.

Once a reply is received, a decision is made:

- If the case needs to be updated, the case details are updated. After updating the case, the work is archived, and then a cancellation is performed. The process then moves to a final decision point.
- If the case does not need to be updated, the process moves to administration. Here, another decision is made:
    - If a request is needed, outstanding documents are requested. After requesting the documents, the case details are updated, the work is archived, and a cancellation is performed. The process then moves to the next decision point.
    - If no request is needed, the process skips directly to the next decision point.

After either path in administration, the process reaches the final decision point. From here, the process ends.
