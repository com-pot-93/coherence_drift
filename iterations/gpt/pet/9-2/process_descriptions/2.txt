The process begins when a mortgage offer is sent. After sending the offer, the process waits for a reply from the customer.

Once a reply is received, a decision is made. If the case needs to be updated, the case details are updated, the work is archived, and a cancellation is performed. After the cancellation, the process moves to another decision point.

If the case does not need to be updated, it is moved to administration. At this stage, another decision is made. If a request for outstanding documents is needed, a request is generated, the case details are updated, the work is archived, and a cancellation is performed. After this, the process moves to the next decision point. If no request for outstanding documents is needed, the process skips directly to the next decision point.

Finally, after either path—whether through updating the case or moving it to administration—the process reaches the final decision point and then ends.
