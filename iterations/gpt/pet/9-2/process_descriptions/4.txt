The process begins when a mortgage offer is sent. After sending the offer, the process waits for a reply from the customer.

Once a reply is received, a decision is made. If the case needs to be updated, the case details are updated. After updating the case details, the work is archived, and then a cancellation is performed. Following the cancellation, the process moves to another decision point.

If the case does not need to be updated, the process continues with administrative tasks. After completing the administration, another decision is made. If a request is needed, outstanding documents are requested. Once the documents are requested, the case details are updated, the work is archived, and a cancellation is performed. After the cancellation, the process moves to the next decision point. If no request is needed, the process skips directly to this decision point.

Finally, after all necessary updates, requests, and cancellations have been handled, the process ends.
