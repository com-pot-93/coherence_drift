The process begins when a mortgage offer is sent. After sending the offer, the process waits for a reply from the customer.

Once a reply is received, a decision is made:

- If the case needs to be updated, the case details are updated. After updating the details, the work is archived, and then a cancellation is performed. Once the cancellation is complete, the process moves to the next decision point.
- If the case does not need to be updated, administrative tasks are performed. After these tasks, another decision is made:
    - If a request is needed, outstanding documents are requested. Once the documents are requested, the case details are updated, the work is archived, and a cancellation is performed. After the cancellation, the process moves to the next decision point.
    - If no request is needed, the process moves directly to the next decision point.

At the final decision point, the process concludes and ends.
