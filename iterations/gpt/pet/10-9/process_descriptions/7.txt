The process begins when an initial event occurs. At this point, there are two possible ways the process can start:

- If the process is started by an external party, the external party submits a change request.
- If the process is started by the MPO, the MPO begins the change process.

Regardless of how the process starts, the next step is for the MPO to review the change request.

After the review, there are two possible outcomes:

- If the request is rejected, the MPO formally rejects the request.
- If the request is confirmed, the MPO confirms the request.

Once either the rejection or confirmation is completed, the MPO proceeds to carry out the change.

After attempting to carry out the change, there are again two possible results:

- If the change is successful, the MPO reports the successful implementation.
- If the change fails, the MPO notifies the relevant parties about the failure.

After reporting either a successful implementation or a failure, the process comes to an end.
