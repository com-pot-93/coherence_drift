The process begins with a starting point. From there, it can proceed in one of two ways:

- If the process is initiated by an external party, the external party submits a change request.
- If the process is initiated by the MPO, the MPO begins the change process.

Regardless of how it starts, the next step is for the MPO to review the change request.

After the review, there are two possible outcomes:

- If the request is rejected, the MPO rejects the request.
- If the request is confirmed, the MPO confirms the request.

In either case, the process continues to the next stage, where the MPO carries out the change.

Once the change is carried out, there are again two possible results:

- If the change is successful, the MPO reports the success.
- If the change fails, the MPO notifies about the failure.

After reporting either success or failure, the process comes to an end.
