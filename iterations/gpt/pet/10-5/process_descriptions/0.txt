The process begins when the MPON asks the MPOO to submit a bid for taking over a device. In response, the MPOO sends a tender to the MPON, outlining the terms for the equipment takeover. After receiving the tender, the MPON places an order with the MPOO. The MPOO then confirms that it has received the order from the MPON and provides the necessary master data. Once this is done, the process comes to an end.
