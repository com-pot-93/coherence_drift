The process begins with the start of the procedure. First, a bid request is sent to the MPOO. After this, a tender is sent to the MPON. Once the tender has been sent, an order is placed with the MPOO.

After placing the order, two activities happen at the same time. One activity is to confirm the order, and the other is to provide master data. Both of these actions are carried out independently and simultaneously.

Once both the order has been confirmed and the master data has been provided, the process comes to an end.
