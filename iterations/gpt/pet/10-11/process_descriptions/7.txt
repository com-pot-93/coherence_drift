The process begins when a request to start a measurement is initiated. This request is then received and reviewed. After the review, a decision is made:

- If the request is denied, the person or group known as "GO" is informed about the denial. After this notification, the process moves towards completion.
- If the request is approved, the measurement is performed. Once the measurement is attempted, another decision is made:
    - If the measurement fails, "GO" is notified about the failure, and the process continues towards completion.
    - If the measurement is successful, the measured values are sent to "GO".

After "GO" receives the measured values, these values are processed. At this point, another decision is made:

- If the values have changed as a result of processing, the updated values are sent to the "MSP".
- If there is no change in the values, this step is skipped.

Regardless of whether the values were updated or not, the readings are then forwarded to the "SP". After this, the process moves towards completion.

The process ends after all necessary notifications and forwarding of readings have been completed.
