The process begins with a starting point. First, a decision is made about whether to go public. Once this decision is made, the next step is to select underwriters who will assist with the process. After the underwriters are chosen, a registration statement is prepared.

Following the preparation, the registration statement is submitted. Once submitted, the statement is reviewed. After the review, there is a check to ensure all compliance requirements are met.

At this stage, three activities are carried out at the same time:

1. The final issue price is determined.
2. Preparations are made for the road show.
3. The process waits for regulatory approval.

Once all three of these activities are completed, the process continues. Regulatory clearance is then received. After receiving clearance, the final issue price is set. Next, a firm commitment is made to proceed.

Following this, the stock is offered to the public. The process concludes at this point.
