The process begins with an initial step. First, a backup of the database is created. After the backup is complete, the database is checked.

At this point, a decision is made: either to continue or to skip the next step.  
- If the decision is to continue, the CRM system is checked.  
- If the decision is to skip, the process moves directly to the next decision point.

After checking the CRM system (if that path was chosen), the process reaches another decision point. Here, there is again a choice to continue or to skip.  
- If the decision is to continue, defaulting accounts and customers are registered.  
- If the decision is to skip, the process moves to the next decision point.

Once defaulting accounts and customers have been registered (if that path was chosen), the process reaches another decision point. Again, there is a choice to continue or to skip.  
- If the decision is to continue, a table of defaulting accounts is produced. This table is then sent to account management.  
- If the decision is to skip, the process moves to the next decision point.

After the table has been sent to account management (if that path was chosen), the process reaches another decision point. Here, a choice is made between sending an alert to the supervisor or not.  
- If an alert is needed, an alert is sent to the supervisor.  
- If no alert is needed, the process moves to the next step.

After this, the CRM system is checked again.

Next, another decision is made: whether reconciliation is needed or not.  
- If reconciliation is needed, reconciliation is performed.  
- If no reconciliation is needed, the process moves to the next decision point.

After reconciliation is performed (if that path was chosen), the process reaches another decision point with three possible actions:  
- A message can be sent to the supervisor.  
- Reconciliation can be completed.  
- The process can simply continue.

If a message is sent to the supervisor or reconciliation is completed, the process continues to the next step. If the process simply continues, it also moves to the next step.

Finally, the last step of the process is performed, and the process ends.
