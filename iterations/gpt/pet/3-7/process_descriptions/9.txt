The process begins when an initial event occurs. First, a search is conducted to locate the police report related to the case. Once the police report has been found, the next step is to gather all relevant case documentation. After collecting the necessary documents, the information is carefully reviewed to ensure everything is accurate and complete.

Following the review, an estimate is calculated based on the information gathered. With the estimate in hand, an action plan is developed to address the situation. The process then moves on to negotiating a settlement, where terms are discussed and agreed upon.

Once the settlement has been negotiated, the claimant is informed of the outcome. The process concludes after the claimant has been notified.
