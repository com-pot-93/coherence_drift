The process begins. First, a search is conducted to find the police report related to the case. Once the police report has been located, the next step is to gather all necessary documentation for the claim. After collecting the required documents, the information is carefully reviewed to ensure everything is accurate and complete.

Following the review, an estimate is calculated based on the gathered information. With the estimate in hand, an action plan is developed to address the claim. The process then moves to negotiating a settlement, where terms are discussed and agreed upon.

After reaching a settlement, the claimant is informed of the outcome. Once the claimant has been notified, the process comes to an end.
