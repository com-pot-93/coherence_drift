The process begins. First, a search is conducted to locate the police report. Once the police report has been found, the next step is to gather all necessary documentation related to the case. After collecting the documentation, the information is carefully reviewed to ensure everything is accurate and complete.

Following the review, an estimate is calculated based on the information that has been gathered and reviewed. With the estimate in hand, an action plan is developed to address the situation. The next step is to negotiate a settlement, using the action plan as a guide.

Once a settlement has been negotiated, the claimant is informed about the outcome. After the claimant has been notified, the process comes to an end.
