The process begins when the work starts. First, a search is conducted to find the relevant police report. Once the police report has been located, the next step is to gather all the necessary case documentation. After collecting the documentation, the information is carefully reviewed to ensure that it is accurate and complete.

Following this review, an estimate is calculated based on the information available. With the estimate in hand, an action plan is developed to address the situation. The next step involves negotiating a settlement, using the action plan as a guide.

Once the negotiation is complete, the claimant is informed of the outcome. After the claimant has been notified, the process comes to an end.
