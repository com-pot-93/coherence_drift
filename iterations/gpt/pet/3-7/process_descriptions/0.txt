The process begins when an employee starts by searching for the police report. Once the police report has been found, the employee gathers all the necessary claim documentation along with the police report. After this, a claims handler reviews the collected information and calculates an initial estimate for the claim.

Next, the claims handler develops an action plan based on the initial estimate. Following this, a claims manager takes over and negotiates a settlement using the claim estimate as a basis. Once the negotiation is complete, the claims manager communicates the outcome to the claimant. The process concludes after the claimant has been informed of the result.
