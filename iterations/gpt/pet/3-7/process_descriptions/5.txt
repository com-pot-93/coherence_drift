The process begins. First, a search is conducted for the police report. After the police report is found, the next step is to gather all necessary documentation. Once the documentation has been collected, the information is carefully reviewed. Following the review, an estimate is calculated based on the available information. After the estimate is calculated, an action plan is developed. Once the action plan is ready, a negotiation takes place to reach a settlement. After the settlement has been negotiated, the claimant is informed about the outcome. The process then ends.
