The process begins when a search is conducted to locate the relevant police report. Once the police report has been found, the next step is to gather all necessary case documentation. After collecting the documentation, the information is carefully reviewed to ensure that it is accurate and complete.

Following this review, an estimate is calculated based on the available information. With the estimate in place, an action plan is developed to address the situation. The process then moves on to negotiating a settlement.

Once the negotiation is complete, the claimant is informed of the outcome. The process concludes after the claimant has been notified.
