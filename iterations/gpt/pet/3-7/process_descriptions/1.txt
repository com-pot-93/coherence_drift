The process begins when the work starts. First, a search is conducted to find the relevant police report. Once the police report has been located, the next step is to gather all necessary documentation related to the claim.

After collecting the required documents, the information is carefully reviewed, and an estimate is calculated based on the findings. With this estimate in hand, an action plan is developed to address the claim.

Following the creation of the action plan, negotiations are carried out to reach a settlement. Once a settlement has been negotiated, the outcome is communicated to the person who made the claim.

The process concludes after the claimant has been informed of the outcome.
