The process begins. First, a search is conducted to find the police report. Once the police report has been located, the next step is to gather all necessary documentation related to the case. After collecting the documentation, the information is carefully reviewed. Following the review, an estimate is calculated based on the available information. With the estimate in hand, an action plan is developed to address the situation. The next step involves negotiating a settlement. Once the negotiation is complete, the claimant is informed about the outcome. The process then ends.
