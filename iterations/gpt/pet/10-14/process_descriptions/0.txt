The process begins. First, a decision is made about how to proceed. There are two possible paths from this point.

**First path:**
- Another decision is made, leading to four possible actions:
  1. The metering point operations organization sends the metering point operations bill to the grid operator.
  2. The measurement service provider sends the measurement bill to the grid operator.
  3. The measurement service provider sends the bill for additional readings to the grid operator.
  4. The metering point operations organization sends the bill for equipment acquisition to the grid operator.
- After any of these actions, the grid operator examines the bill.
- The grid operator then decides whether to confirm or reject the invoice:
  - If the invoice is rejected, the grid operator rejects the invoice.
  - If the invoice is confirmed, the grid operator confirms the invoice and provides payment advice.
- After either confirming or rejecting the invoice, the process continues to the next step.

**Second path:**
- The metering point operations organization sends the bill for equipment acquisition to the metering point operator network.
- The metering point operator network examines the bill.
- The metering point operator network then decides whether to confirm or reject the invoice:
  - If the invoice is confirmed, the metering point operator network confirms the invoice and provides payment advice.
  - If the invoice is rejected, the metering point operator network rejects the invoice.
- After either confirming or rejecting the invoice, the process continues to the next step.

Finally, after all actions and decisions are completed, the process ends.
