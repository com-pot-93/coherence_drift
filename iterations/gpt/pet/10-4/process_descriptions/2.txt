The process begins when MPON informs MPOO about their intention to change equipment. After this notification, MPOO decides whether they will remove their own equipment or not.

If MPOO chooses to remove their own equipment, they announce this decision to MPON. If MPOO decides not to remove their own equipment, they inform MPON of this choice. Regardless of the decision, the process continues to the next step.

Next, it must be determined who is responsible for performing the final reading. If MPON is responsible, MPON performs the final reading. If MPOO is responsible, MPOO performs the final reading. After the final reading is completed by the responsible party, the process moves forward.

The following step is to decide who will remove the old equipment. If MPON is to remove the old equipment, they proceed to do so. If MPOO is responsible, they remove the old equipment. Once the old equipment has been removed by the appropriate party, the process continues.

After the old equipment is removed, MPON installs the new device. Once the new device is installed, MPON reads the meter count from the new meter. MPON then sends the final readings and relevant data to GO.

GO, upon receiving the data, notifies MSP about the required data. This completes the process.
