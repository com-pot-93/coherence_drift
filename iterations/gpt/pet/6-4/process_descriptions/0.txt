The process begins when the company identifies the contact details of potential customers. After this, the company chooses one of three ways to proceed:

1. The company may purchase contact details specifically for making cold calls.
2. Marketing staff may participate in exhibitions to gather contact information.
3. The company may already know a person who is interested in the product.

Regardless of which of these three paths is chosen, the process continues in the same way. The call center staff then calls the customer. During this call, the staff first determines who the appropriate contact person is, then finds out the customer’s budget, and finally assesses the customer’s level of interest in the product.
