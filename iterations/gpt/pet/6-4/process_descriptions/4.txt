The process begins with the identification of potential customers. Once potential customers have been identified, there are three possible ways to obtain their contact information:

1. If the approach is to make cold calls, the contact details are purchased.
2. If the approach is through an exhibition, contact information is collected at the exhibition.
3. If there is already an existing interested contact, that contact is used.

After obtaining the contact information through one of these three methods, the next step is to call the customer. Following the call, the appropriate contact person within the customer’s organization is determined. Once the right contact person has been identified, the customer’s budget is found out.

At this point, there are two possible paths:

1. If the process is to proceed with sales, sales or pre-sales activities are carried out.
2. If the head of development is to acquire the customer, the head of development takes over the acquisition.

After either of these actions is completed, the process comes to an end.
