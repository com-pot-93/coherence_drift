The process begins when a dismissal is received from MSPN. After this, the dismissal is reviewed by MSPO. Based on the outcome of this review, there are two possible paths:

- If the dismissal is rejected, the process continues with the rejection of the dismissal.
- If the dismissal is confirmed, the process continues with the confirmation of the dismissal.

Regardless of whether the dismissal is rejected or confirmed, the process then moves to the final step and ends.
