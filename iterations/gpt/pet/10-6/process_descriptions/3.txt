The process begins when a dismissal is received from MSPN. After receiving the dismissal, the next step is for MSPO to review the dismissal. Once the review is complete, a decision is made.

If the dismissal is rejected, the process continues with the rejection of the dismissal. After this, the process moves forward to the next decision point.

If the dismissal is confirmed, the process continues with the confirmation of the dismissal. After this, the process also moves forward to the same decision point as above.

Regardless of whether the dismissal was rejected or confirmed, the process then proceeds to a final step. Once the final step is completed, the process ends.
