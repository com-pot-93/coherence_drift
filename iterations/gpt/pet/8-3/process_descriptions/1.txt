The process begins when a need for personnel is identified. First, the necessary personnel requirements are determined. Once these requirements are clear, the vacancy is reported to the personnel department.

At this point, a decision is made about whether to proceed further. If the process continues, the next step is to receive the job description for review. The job description is then carefully examined.

After examining the job description, another decision is made. If the job description is not satisfactory, corrections are requested. Once corrections are made, the process returns to the point where the job description is received for review, and the cycle of review and correction continues until the job description is satisfactory.

When the job description is deemed satisfactory, the vacancy is advertised. After advertising the vacancy, the process returns to the earlier decision point, allowing for the possibility of handling additional vacancies or requirements. If there are no further actions to take, the process comes to an end.
