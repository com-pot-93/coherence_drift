The process begins when a loan request is received. After receiving the request, the next step is to assess the risk associated with the loan application.

Once the risk has been assessed, there are three possible outcomes:

1. If the application is approved, approval feedback is sent to the applicant.
2. If the application is denied, denial feedback is sent to the applicant.
3. If further review is needed, a request for further review is made, and then review feedback is sent to the applicant.

After any of these feedback messages are sent—whether it is approval feedback, denial feedback, or review feedback—the process comes to an end.
