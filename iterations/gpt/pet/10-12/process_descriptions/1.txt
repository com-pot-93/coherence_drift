The process begins when EC informs INQ about a change in data. After receiving this information, INQ notifies IP about the change. IP then reviews the situation to assess the proposed change.

Following the review, IP decides whether the change can be made. If IP determines that the change can be made, IP confirms the changes to INQ. If IP decides that the change cannot be made, IP rejects the changes to INQ.

After either confirming or rejecting the changes, the process comes to an end.
