The process begins when an order is received from the sales team. After receiving the order, the next step is to determine which parts are needed, how many of each part are required, and the delivery dates for these parts. Once this information is established, it is entered into the production planning system.

Following this, a parts list is generated based on the entered information. This parts list is then transferred to the ERP system. At this point, there is a check to see if any parts are missing.

If it is found that some parts are missing, the process continues with procuring the missing parts. Once this is done, or if no parts were missing in the first place, the process moves forward.

The next step is to receive a notification from the system. After receiving this notification, the necessary steps indicated by the system are performed. Once these steps are completed, the order quality is checked. The process concludes after the order quality has been checked.
