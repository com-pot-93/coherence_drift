The process begins when an order is received from the sales team. After receiving the order, the next step is to determine which parts are needed, how many of each part are required, and the delivery dates for these parts. Once this information is established, it is entered into the production planning system.

Following this, a list of the required parts is generated. This parts list is then transferred to the ERP system. The process continues with a check to see if any parts are missing.

At this point, there are two possible paths:

- If parts are missing, the missing parts are procured. After procuring the missing parts, the process continues to the next step.
- If no parts are missing, the process moves directly to the next step.

After ensuring that all parts are accounted for, a system notification is received. The indicated steps in the notification are then performed. Once these steps are completed, the order quality is checked. The process concludes after the order quality check.
