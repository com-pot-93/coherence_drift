The process begins when an employee submits a vacation request. After the request is submitted, it is registered in the system. Next, the supervisor reviews the request.

At this point, there are two possible outcomes based on the supervisor’s decision:

- If the supervisor does not approve the request, the application is returned to the employee. After this, the process moves to the final step.
- If the supervisor approves the request, the human resources representative is notified. Then, the necessary HR procedures are completed. After these steps, the process also moves to the final step.

In both cases, the process ends after either the application is returned to the employee or the HR procedures are completed.
