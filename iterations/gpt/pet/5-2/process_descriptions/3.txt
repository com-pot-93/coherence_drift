The process begins when an employee submits a vacation request. Once the request is submitted, it is registered in the system. After registration, the supervisor receives the request. The supervisor then reviews the vacation request.

Following the review, there are two possible outcomes:

- If the request is not approved, the application is returned to the employee. After this, the process moves towards completion.
- If the request is approved, the human resources representative is notified. The HR representative then completes the necessary HR procedure. After this, the process also moves towards completion.

In both cases, whether the request is approved or not, the process ends after the respective actions are taken.
