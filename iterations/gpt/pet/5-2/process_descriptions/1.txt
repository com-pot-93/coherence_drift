The process begins when an employee submits a vacation request. After the request is submitted, it is registered in the system. Once registered, the supervisor receives the request. The supervisor then reviews the request and makes a decision.

If the supervisor does not approve the request, the application is returned to the employee. After this, the process comes to an end.

If the supervisor approves the request, the HR representative is notified. The HR representative then completes the necessary procedure. Once this is done, the process comes to an end.
