The process begins when an employee submits a vacation request. After the request is submitted, it is registered in the system. Once the request is registered, the supervisor receives it. The supervisor then reviews the vacation request.

After the review, there are two possible outcomes:

- If the supervisor does not approve the request, the application is returned to the employee.  
- If the supervisor approves the request, the human resources representative is notified. The HR representative then completes the necessary procedure.

Regardless of whether the application was returned to the employee or the HR procedure was completed, the process comes to an end.
