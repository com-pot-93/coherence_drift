The process begins when an employee submits a vacation request. After the request is submitted, it is registered in the system. Next, the supervisor reviews the request.

At this point, there are two possible outcomes based on the supervisor’s decision:

- If the request is not approved, the application is returned to the employee.
- If the request is approved, the human resources representative is notified, and the necessary HR procedures are completed.

After either the application is returned to the employee or the HR procedures are completed, the process comes to an end.
