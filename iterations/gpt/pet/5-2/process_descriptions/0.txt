The process begins when an employee submits a vacation request. After the request is submitted, the requirement is registered. Next, the immediate supervisor receives the request. The supervisor then reviews and decides whether to approve the request.

At this point, there are two possible outcomes:

- If the supervisor does not approve the request, the system returns the application to the employee.
- If the supervisor approves the request, a notification is generated for the HR representative.

If the application is returned to the employee, the process moves directly toward completion. If a notification is sent to the HR representative, the HR representative completes the necessary procedure, after which the process also moves toward completion.

In both cases, whether the application is returned to the employee or the HR representative completes the procedure, the process ends.
