The process begins when the patient arrives. After the patient’s arrival, the room is prepared for surgery. Once the room is ready, the surgery is performed.

After the surgery is completed, two activities happen at the same time: the patient is discharged, and the room is sanitized. Both of these actions must be finished before the process continues.

Once the patient has been discharged and the room has been sanitized, a report is filled out. After the report is completed, it is determined whether payment is required. If payment is required, the bill is paid. If no payment is required, the process continues without this step.

Finally, the process ends.
