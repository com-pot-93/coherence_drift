The process begins when a customer request is received. After receiving the request, the details of the request are checked.

At this point, a decision is made about whether to continue with the process. If the decision is not to continue, the process activity is ended, and the process moves towards completion.

If the process continues, another decision is made to determine how the registration should be handled. If manual registration is chosen, the registration is completed manually. If automatic registration is chosen, the registration is completed automatically. After registration, the process continues.

Next, a decision is made about whether to search for an attraction provider. If the decision is not to search, a notification is sent to the customer, and the process moves towards completion.

If the decision is to search for an attraction provider, the search is performed. After searching, another decision is made about whether to proceed with booking. If the decision is not to book, the process activity is ended, and the process moves towards completion.

If the decision is to attempt booking, the booking is attempted. After the booking attempt, a decision is made based on the outcome. If the booking is rejected, the rejection is received, and the process moves towards completion. If the booking is confirmed, the confirmation is received, and a confirmation is sent to the customer. After this, the process moves towards completion.

Finally, the process ends.
