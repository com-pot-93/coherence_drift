The process begins. Immediately, several activities are carried out at the same time:

- Information about profiling logic is added.
- Information about the right to rectify data is added.
- Information about the right to access data is added.
- Information about the right to erase data is added.
- Information about data portability is added.
- Information about withdrawing consent is added.
- Information about lodging a complaint is added.
- The legal basis for processing is retrieved.
- The purposes for processing are retrieved.
- The identity and contact details are retrieved.

Once all of these activities are completed, the process continues by collecting consent information. After that, consent is requested. When this is done, the process continues to the next step. Finally, the process ends.
