The process begins with a starting point. Immediately after this, several activities are carried out at the same time:

- Information about profiling logic is added.
- Information about the right to rectify is added.
- Information about the right to access is added.
- Information about the right to erase is added.
- Information about data portability is added.
- Information about withdrawing consent is added.
- Information about the complaint procedure is added.
- The legal basis for processing is retrieved.
- The purposes for processing are retrieved.
- The identity and contact details are retrieved.

All of these activities happen independently and simultaneously. Once every one of these tasks is completed, the process continues to the next step.

After all the previous information has been gathered and added, consent information is collected. Following this, a request for consent is made.

The process ends after the consent has been requested.
