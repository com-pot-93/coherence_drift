The process begins when a data subject request is received. After receiving the request, the next step is to retrieve the relevant data associated with the request. Once the data has been retrieved, it is checked to determine its relevance.

At this point, there are two possible outcomes:

- If the data is still relevant, an explanation is prepared regarding the data.  
- If the data is no longer relevant, the data is eliminated, and a notification about the elimination is sent.

After either preparing the explanation or sending the elimination notification, the process comes to an end.
