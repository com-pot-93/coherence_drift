The process begins when a data subject request is received. After receiving the request, the next step is to retrieve the relevant data associated with it. Once the data has been gathered, it is checked to determine its relevance.

At this point, there is a decision based on whether the data is relevant or not:

- If the data is found to be relevant, an explanation is prepared. After preparing the explanation, the process continues to the next step.
- If the data is not relevant, the data is eliminated. Once the data has been eliminated, a notification about the elimination is sent. After sending this notification, the process continues to the next step.

Regardless of which path was taken—either preparing an explanation or eliminating the data and sending a notification—the process then comes to an end.
