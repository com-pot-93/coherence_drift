The process begins when a data subject request is received. After receiving the request, the next step is to retrieve the relevant data associated with it. Once the data has been gathered, it is checked to determine its relevance.

At this point, there are two possible paths:

- If the data is found to be relevant, an explanation is prepared.  
- If the data is not relevant, the data is eliminated, and a notification about the elimination is sent.

After either preparing the explanation or sending the elimination notification, the process comes to an end.
