The process begins when a trip request is received. Once the request is received, two activities are started at the same time.

First, a price request is sent to airlines. After sending the request, the responses from the airlines are collected. Once all airline responses have been gathered, an airline is selected.

At the same time as the airline request, a price request is also sent to hotels. After sending this request, the responses from the hotels are collected. Once all hotel responses have been gathered, a hotel is selected.

After both the airline and hotel have been selected, the process continues by preparing a tour plan. Once the tour plan is ready, it is sent to the customer. The process ends after the tour plan has been sent to the customer.
