The process begins when a trip request is received. Once the request is received, two activities are started at the same time.

The first activity is to send a price request to the airlines. After sending the request, responses from the airlines are collected. Once all responses are gathered, an airline is selected.

The second activity is to send a price request to the hotels. After sending this request, responses from the hotels are collected. Once all responses are gathered, a hotel is selected.

After both the airline and the hotel have been selected, the process continues by preparing the tour plan. Once the tour plan is ready, it is sent to the customer. The process ends after the tour plan has been sent to the customer.
