The process begins when a trip request is received. Once the request is received, two activities are started at the same time.

First, a price request is sent to airlines. After sending the request, airline responses are collected. Once all responses from the airlines have been gathered, an airline is selected.

At the same time as the airline process, a price request is also sent to hotels. After sending the request, hotel responses are collected. Once all responses from the hotels have been gathered, a hotel is selected.

After both the airline and hotel have been selected, the process continues by preparing a tour plan. Once the tour plan is ready, it is sent to the customer. The process ends after the tour plan has been sent to the customer.
