The process begins when a trip request is received. After receiving the trip request, two activities are carried out at the same time. One activity involves sending a price request to airlines, while the other involves sending a price request to hotels.

For the airline part, after the price request is sent to airlines, the responses from the airlines are collected. Once all airline responses are gathered, an airline is selected.

For the hotel part, after the price request is sent to hotels, the responses from the hotels are collected. Once all hotel responses are gathered, a hotel is selected.

After both an airline and a hotel have been selected, the process continues by preparing a tour plan. Once the tour plan is ready, it is sent to the customer. The process ends after the tour plan has been sent to the customer.
