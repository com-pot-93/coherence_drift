The process begins when a trip request is received. After receiving the trip request, two activities are started at the same time.

First, a price request is sent to airlines. Once the price request has been sent, airline responses are collected. After collecting the responses from the airlines, an airline is selected.

At the same time as the airline process, a price request is also sent to hotels. Once the price request has been sent, hotel responses are collected. After collecting the responses from the hotels, a hotel is selected.

Once both an airline and a hotel have been selected, the process continues. A tour plan is prepared based on the selections. After the tour plan is prepared, it is sent to the customer. The process ends after the tour plan has been sent to the customer.
