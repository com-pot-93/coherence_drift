The process begins with the start of the event. First, invitations are sent to friends. After the invitations are sent, game suggestions are requested from the friends. Once the suggestions are requested, the process continues by receiving the game suggestions.

After receiving the suggestions, the next step is to select which games will be played. Once the games are selected, there is a decision point: it is determined whether any of the selected games need to be downloaded.

If downloads are needed, the necessary games are downloaded. If no downloads are needed, the process skips this step. In both cases, the process continues to the next stage.

Next, friends are asked if they need any beer. After gathering this information, an estimate is made of the total amount of beer required for the party. At this point, another decision is made: it is checked whether more beer needs to be bought.

If more beer is needed, additional beer is purchased. If there is already enough beer, this step is skipped. Regardless of the outcome, the process then moves on to the final party preparations.

Once the final preparations are complete, the process ends.
