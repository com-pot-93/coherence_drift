The process begins with a starting point. The first step is to collect requirements. After the requirements have been collected, a decision is made about whether to continue. If the decision is to continue, the requirements are sent forward.

Once the requirements have been sent forward, the next step is to receive a draft. After receiving the draft, another decision is made about whether to continue. If the decision is to continue, a further condition is checked. If this condition is met, the draft is evaluated.

After evaluating the draft, another decision is made about whether to proceed. If the decision is to proceed, the process continues through a series of checkpoints, one after another, until it reaches the end point, where the process finishes.
