The process begins with an initial action being performed. After this, a decision is made regarding the registration status of the car. If the car is not registered, the process moves directly to the next step, which is followed by the end of the process.

If the car is registered, the car owner is notified. Following this notification, another decision is made based on whether the car is brought to the mechanic. If the car is not brought to the mechanic, an alternative action is performed, and then a fine is issued to the car owner. After issuing the fine, the process continues to the next step, which leads to the end of the process.

If the car is brought to the mechanic, information about the car problem is received. After this, a payment offer is made. At this point, a decision is made about transferring the payment. If the payment is transferred, the payment is sent to the mechanic. If the payment is not transferred, the process continues without this step. In both cases, the process proceeds to receive confirmation that the repair has been completed. After receiving this confirmation, the next service time is received. The process then continues to the next step, which leads to the end of the process.
