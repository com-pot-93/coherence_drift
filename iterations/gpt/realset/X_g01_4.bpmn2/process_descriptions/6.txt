The process begins with the initiation of the procedure. After this, a decision is made about whether to proceed. If the decision is not to proceed, the process ends immediately.

If the decision is to continue, the next step is to select a course. After selecting a course, another decision is made about whether to stop. If the choice is to stop, the process ends.

If the process continues, the available dates for the course are shown, and then a date is selected. After selecting a date, there is another decision point to determine whether to stop. If the process is stopped here, it ends.

If the process continues, the account status is checked. At this point, there are two possible paths:

1. **If the account is in good standing:**  
   - The user is prompted to log in.
   - After logging in, the user provides payment information.
   - Once payment information is provided, registration is completed.
   - After completing registration, there is a decision about whether to stop. If the process is stopped, it ends.
   - If the process continues, two actions happen at the same time:  
     - A message is sent to friends.
     - A ticket is received.
   - Once both actions are completed, the process ends.

2. **If the account is not in good standing:**  
   - The university eligibility is checked.
   - After checking eligibility, there are three possible paths:
     - **If eligible:**  
       - The user registers for the course.
       - After registration, there is a decision about whether registration failed. If registration failed, the process ends.
       - If registration is successful, registration is completed.
       - After completing registration, there is a decision about whether to stop. If the process is stopped, it ends.
       - If the process continues, two actions happen at the same time:  
         - A message is sent to friends.
         - A ticket is received.
       - Once both actions are completed, the process ends.
     - **If not eligible:**  
       - An activation request is sent.
       - The process waits for an activation response.
       - After receiving the response, there is a decision about whether activation failed. If activation failed, the process ends.
       - If activation is successful, registration is completed.
       - After completing registration, there is a decision about whether to stop. If the process is stopped, it ends.
       - If the process continues, two actions happen at the same time:  
         - A message is sent to friends.
         - A ticket is received.
       - Once both actions are completed, the process ends.

At every major step—after selecting a course, selecting a date, completing registration, or after certain checks—there are opportunities to stop the process, which leads directly to the end. The process always concludes with an end event, either after a stop decision or after all required actions are completed.
