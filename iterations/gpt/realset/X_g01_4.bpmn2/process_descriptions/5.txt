The process begins with the initiation of the procedure. After this, a decision is made on whether to proceed. If the decision is not to proceed, the process ends immediately.

If the process continues, the next step is to select a course. After selecting a course, another decision is made on whether to continue. If the decision is to stop, the process ends.

If the process continues, the available dates for the selected course are shown. The user then selects a date. After the date is selected, another decision is made on whether to proceed. If the decision is to stop, the process ends.

If the process continues, the account status is checked. Based on the result, there are two possible paths:

1. **If the account status requires logging in:**
   - The user logs in.
   - The user provides payment information.
   - The registration is completed.
   - After completing registration, a decision is made on whether to continue. If the decision is to stop, the process ends.
   - If the process continues, two actions happen at the same time: a message is sent to friends on social media, and a ticket is received. Once both actions are finished, the process continues.
   - The process then moves to the next decision point.

2. **If the account status requires checking university eligibility:**
   - University eligibility is checked.
   - A decision is made based on the eligibility check:
     - If eligible, the user registers for the course.
     - After registration, another decision is made:
       - If registration fails, the process ends.
       - If registration is successful, the registration is completed.
       - After completing registration, a decision is made on whether to continue. If the decision is to stop, the process ends.
       - If the process continues, two actions happen at the same time: a message is sent to friends on social media, and a ticket is received. Once both actions are finished, the process continues.
     - If not eligible, an activation request is sent.
     - The process waits for an activation response.
     - After receiving the response, a decision is made:
       - If activation fails, the process ends.
       - If activation is successful, the registration is completed.
       - After completing registration, a decision is made on whether to continue. If the decision is to stop, the process ends.
       - If the process continues, two actions happen at the same time: a message is sent to friends on social media, and a ticket is received. Once both actions are finished, the process continues.

After any of these paths are completed, the process returns to the previous decision points and continues until all possible actions are finished. Finally, the process ends.
