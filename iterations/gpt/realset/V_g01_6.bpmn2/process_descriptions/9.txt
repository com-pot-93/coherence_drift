The process begins when someone initiates a request. The first step is to ask whether the person would like to order a burger or see the menu. Based on the response, the process follows one of two paths:

- If the person chooses "Burger," the process continues by handling the burger order.
- If the person chooses "Menu," the process continues by handling the menu order.

After either the burger order is processed or the menu order is processed, the process comes back together and then ends.
