The process begins when information about the fridge’s symptoms and its type is received. After this information is collected, a service appointment is scheduled. Once the appointment is set, a technician inspects the fridge. Following the inspection, there is a step to confirm that the fridge is working properly. After confirming that everything is functioning as it should, a request is made for a facility rating. The user then submits their rating. The process ends after the user’s rating has been submitted.
