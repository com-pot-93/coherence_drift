The process begins when someone initiates the procedure. First, the person sends information about the symptoms and the type of fridge to the service center. After this information is sent, an appointment is made for a service visit.

Once the appointment is scheduled, the fridge is checked according to the agreed time. After the check is completed, it is confirmed whether the fridge is working properly.

Following this confirmation, a request is made for a rating of the facility. The person then provides their rating. Once the rating is given, the process comes to an end.
