The process begins when information about the fridge’s symptoms and its type is received. After this information is collected, a service appointment is scheduled. Once the appointment is set, a technician inspects the fridge. Following the inspection, there is a step to confirm that the fridge is working properly. After confirming the fridge’s proper functioning, a request is made for a facility rating. The user then provides their rating for the facility. The process concludes after the user submits their rating.
