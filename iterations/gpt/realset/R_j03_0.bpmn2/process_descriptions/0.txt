The process begins when someone initiates the procedure. First, the person sends information about the symptoms and the type of fridge to the service center. After this information is sent, an appointment is made for further action. Once the appointment is set, the fridge is checked as scheduled. Following the inspection, there is a step to confirm that the fridge is working properly. After confirming that the fridge is in good condition, the person is asked to rate the facility. Once the rating is provided, the process comes to an end.
