The process begins. Two activities are carried out at the same time: one is to check if the baby is younger than two years, and the other is to identify the parent. Once both of these activities are completed, the process continues.

Next, a decision is made. If certain conditions are met, a notification is sent to the employer. After this, the process waits for confirmation from the employer. Once confirmation is received, the parent takes maternity leave.

After the parent takes maternity leave, another decision is made based on how the leave is split or extended. There are several possible paths:

1. **If the leave is split and the split is less than two months:**  
   The employer is notified about the split being less than two months. The process then waits for a notification from the employer regarding this split. After receiving the notification, the parent takes leave following the split of less than two months.

2. **If the leave is split and the split is more than three months:**  
   The employer is notified about the split being more than three months. The process waits for a notification from the employer about this split. After receiving the notification, the parent takes leave following the split of more than three months.

3. **If the leave is extended and the extension is less than two months:**  
   The employer is notified about the extension being less than two months. The process waits for a notification from the employer regarding this extension. After receiving the notification, the parent takes leave following the extension of less than two months.

4. **If the leave is extended and the extension is more than three months:**  
   The employer is notified about the extension being more than three months. The process waits for a notification from the employer about this extension. After receiving the notification, the parent takes leave following the extension of more than three months.

5. **If both parents take leave together:**  
   Both parents take leave at the same time.

6. **If none of the above situations apply:**  
   The process continues without any additional action.

After any of these paths are completed, the process moves through a series of steps that bring all the possible outcomes together. Finally, the process ends.
