The process begins when an application is received. After receiving the application, two activities are carried out at the same time: the application is confirmed, and the application is rated. Both of these actions happen independently and simultaneously.

Once both the confirmation and the rating of the application are completed, the process continues. At this point, there is a decision to make. If the process should end here, it does so immediately. Otherwise, the process moves forward to the next steps.

If the process continues, an interview is negotiated. After the interview is negotiated, the job seeker is rated. Following this, another decision is made: if the decision is to hire the job seeker, the hiring takes place. If the decision is not to hire, the process skips the hiring step.

Regardless of whether the job seeker is hired or not, the process then moves to a finalization step. After finalizing, the process comes to an end.
