The process begins when an application is received. After receiving the application, two activities are carried out at the same time: the application is confirmed, and the application is rated. Both of these actions must be completed before moving forward.

Once both the confirmation and the rating of the application are finished, the process continues to a decision point. Here, there are two possible paths:

- If the process is to end at this stage, it moves directly to the end of the process.
- If the process is to continue, the next step is to negotiate an interview.

If the interview is negotiated, the next step is to rate the job seeker. After rating the job seeker, another decision is made:

- If the decision is to hire the job seeker, the process proceeds to hiring the job seeker.
- If the decision is not to hire, the process skips the hiring step.

Regardless of whether the job seeker is hired or not, the process then moves to a finalization step, where the process is completed. After this, the process ends.
