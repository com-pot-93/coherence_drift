The process begins when an application is received. After receiving the application, two activities are carried out at the same time: one is to confirm the application, and the other is to rate the application. Both of these activities must be completed before the process continues.

Once both the confirmation and the rating of the application are finished, the process reaches a decision point. At this stage, it is determined whether the process should end or continue. If the decision is to end the process, the process moves directly to the end.

If the process continues, the next step is to negotiate an interview. After the interview is negotiated, the job seeker is rated. Following this, another decision is made: either to hire the job seeker or not.

If the decision is to hire the job seeker, the hiring is carried out. If the decision is not to hire, the process skips the hiring step. In both cases, the process then moves to a finalization step, where the process is completed. After finalization, the process ends.
