The process begins when a job application is received. Once the application is received, two activities take place at the same time: the job application is confirmed, and the job application is rated. Both of these actions are carried out independently and simultaneously.

After both the confirmation and the rating of the job application are completed, the process continues. At this point, a decision is made about how to proceed. There are two possible paths:

1. The process can move directly towards the end without any further action.
2. Alternatively, the process can continue with negotiating an interview with the job applicant. If this path is chosen, after the interview is negotiated, the job seeker is rated.

Following the rating of the job seeker, another decision is made:

- The process can proceed to hiring the prospective customer, after which it continues.
- Or, it can move forward without hiring.

Regardless of which path is taken after rating the job seeker, the process eventually converges and continues towards the end.

Finally, the process concludes.
