The process starts when a customer request is received. The request is then checked. Depending on whether registration is required, the process either proceeds directly to "Continue Process" or goes through a registration step.

If registration is required, there's a choice between manual and automatic registration. After either manual or automatic registration is completed, the process continues to "Continue Process".

After "Continue Process", there is a decision whether to skip searching for an attraction provider. If the search is not skipped, the process searches for an attraction provider. Following the search, there's a check to see if a certain condition is met. If the condition is not met, an attraction is booked. After booking, the booking status is checked.

Based on the booking status, either a confirmation is sent if the booking is confirmed, or a rejection notification is sent if the booking is rejected. After either sending the confirmation or rejection, the process rejoins and proceeds as if the condition was initially met.

If the condition was initially met, or after handling the booking confirmation/rejection, the process proceeds as if the search was skipped.

If the search was skipped initially, or after the condition is met, the process continues with a "Continue" task, followed by sending a notification, and then the process ends.
