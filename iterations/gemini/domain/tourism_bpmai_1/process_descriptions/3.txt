The process starts when a customer request is received. The request is then checked. Depending on the check, the process either proceeds directly or goes through a registration step.

If the process proceeds through the registration step, there is a choice between manual and automatic registration. After either manual or automatic registration, there is an option to search.

If the search is skipped, the process proceeds directly. Otherwise, the process searches for an attraction provider. If a condition is met during the search, the process proceeds. If the condition is not met, an attraction is booked.

After booking the attraction, the booking is either rejected or confirmed. If the booking is confirmed, a confirmation is sent to the customer. Whether the booking is rejected or a confirmation is sent, the process proceeds.

After the search step or if the process proceeds directly from the initial check, a notification is sent to the customer. The process then ends.
