The process starts. Then, two activities, 'Physical Examination' and a blank activity, occur in parallel. After both activities are completed, the process proceeds to 'Make Diagnosis'. Based on the diagnosis, the process either proceeds with 'Surgical Intervention' if the diagnosis is A, or 'Plan Surgery' if the diagnosis is B. After either 'Surgical Intervention' or 'Plan Surgery' is completed, the process continues to 'Discharge Patient', and then the process ends.
