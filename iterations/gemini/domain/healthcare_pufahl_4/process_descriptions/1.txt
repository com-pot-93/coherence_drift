The process starts. The patient's condition is assessed. Then, two activities occur in parallel: a physical examination is performed, and blood samples are drawn, sent to the lab, and the analysis is received. After both the physical examination and receiving the analysis are completed, a diagnosis is made. Based on the diagnosis, one of two exclusive paths is taken. Either a surgical intervention is performed, or surgery is planned. After either the surgical intervention or the surgery planning is completed, the patient is discharged. The process ends.
