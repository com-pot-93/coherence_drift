The process starts. Then, two activities, "Physical Examination" and "Perform Activity," are performed in parallel. After both are completed, the "Make Diagnosis" activity is executed. Subsequently, a decision is made. If "Surgical Intervention needed," then "Surgical Intervention" is performed. If "Plan Surgery needed," then "Plan Surgery" is performed. After either of these activities is completed, the "Discharge Patient" activity is performed, and the process ends.
