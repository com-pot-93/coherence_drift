The process starts. The patient's condition is assessed. Following this, two activities occur in parallel: a physical examination is performed, and blood samples are drawn, sent to the lab, and the analysis is received. Once both the physical examination and the lab analysis are completed, a diagnosis is made. Based on the diagnosis, either a surgical intervention is performed or a surgery is planned. After either the surgical intervention or the surgery planning is complete, the patient is discharged, and the process ends.
