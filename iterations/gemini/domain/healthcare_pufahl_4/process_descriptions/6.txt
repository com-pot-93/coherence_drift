The process starts. Then, two activities, "Physical Examination" and "Perform Activity," are performed in parallel. After both are completed, the "Make Diagnosis" activity is carried out. Subsequently, a decision is made. If "Surgical Intervention needed," then the "Surgical Intervention" activity is performed. If "Plan Surgery needed," the "Plan Surgery" activity is performed. After either of these activities is completed, the "Discharge Patient" activity is performed, and the process ends.
