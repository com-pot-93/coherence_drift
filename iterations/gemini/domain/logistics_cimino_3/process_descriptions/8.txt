The process starts. Then, it splits into two parallel activities: packaging goods and selecting the kind of shipment.

For the "Package Goods" branch: After packaging, a decision is made. If "Request Quotes from Carriers" is chosen, then quotes are requested from carriers, and a carrier is assigned. Another decision is made, and if extra insurance is needed, it is taken out. This decision and insurance step can be repeated.

Alternatively, if "Fill in Post Label" is chosen after packaging, a post label is filled in, and the insurance is checked. If extra insurance is needed, it is taken out. This decision and insurance step can be repeated.

After either of these paths, paperwork is prepared.

For the "Select Kind of Shipment" branch: After selecting the shipment type, a decision is made. If "Request Quotes from Carriers" is chosen, then quotes are requested from carriers, and a carrier is assigned. Another decision is made, and if extra insurance is needed, it is taken out. This decision and insurance step can be repeated.

Alternatively, if "Fill in Post Label" is chosen after selecting the shipment type, a post label is filled in, and the insurance is checked. If extra insurance is needed, it is taken out. This decision and insurance step can be repeated.

After either of these paths, paperwork is prepared.

Once both branches are complete, the paperwork is added, the package is moved to the pickup area, and the process ends.
