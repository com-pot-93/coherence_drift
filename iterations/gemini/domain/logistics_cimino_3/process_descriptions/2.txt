The process starts. Then, it splits into two parallel activities: "Package Goods" and "Select Kind of Shipment." After packaging the goods, the process waits for the "Select Kind of Shipment" activity to complete. Based on the shipment type selected, the process either requests quotes from carriers, assigns a carrier, and prepares paperwork, or fills in a post label and checks insurance. If insurance is required, extra insurance is taken out; otherwise, this step is skipped. After insurance processing or paperwork preparation, the process merges back to a single flow. Once both parallel activities are complete, paperwork is added, the package is moved to the pickup area, and the process ends.
