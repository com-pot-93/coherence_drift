The process starts. Then, it splits into two parallel activities: "Package Goods" and "Select Kind of Shipment," both happening simultaneously. After packaging the goods, the process waits for the shipment kind to be selected. Once the kind of shipment is selected, a decision is made. If quotes are requested, the process proceeds to "Request Quotes from Carriers" and then "Assign Carrier." Otherwise, if quotes are not requested, the process moves directly to "Fill in Post Label," followed by "Check Insurance." After checking insurance, a decision is made whether to "Take out Extra Insurance" or not. Regardless, the process then merges and proceeds to "Prepare Paperwork." After preparing the paperwork, the process waits for the goods to be packaged. Once both parallel activities are completed, the process continues with "Add Paperwork" and then "Move Package to Pickup Area," finally ending the process.
