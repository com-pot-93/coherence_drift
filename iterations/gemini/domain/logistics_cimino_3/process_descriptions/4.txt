The process starts. Then, it splits into two parallel activities: "Package Goods" and "Select Kind of Shipment," both happening simultaneously. After packaging the goods, the process waits for the shipment type to be selected. Once the shipment type is selected, a decision is made. If quotes are requested, the process proceeds to "Request Quotes from Carriers," then "Assign Carrier," and "Prepare Paperwork." Alternatively, if quotes are not requested, the process moves to "Fill in Post Label," then "Check Insurance." If insurance is required, "Take out Extra Insurance" is performed; otherwise, this step is skipped. After insurance is handled or skipped, the process merges back, continuing to "Prepare Paperwork" if the quote path was taken, or waiting until "Prepare Paperwork" is completed if the insurance path was taken. After paperwork is prepared, the process waits for the goods to be packaged. Once both activities are completed, the process continues to "Add Paperwork," then "Move Package to Pickup Area," and finally, the process ends.
