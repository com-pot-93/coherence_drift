The process starts. Then, two activities, "Package Goods" and "Select Kind of Shipment," are performed in parallel.

After "Package Goods" is completed, a decision is made. Depending on the outcome, either "Request Quotes from Carriers" is performed, followed by "Assign Carrier." After "Assign Carrier," another decision is made, leading to a merge point. From the merge point, depending on the outcome, either "Take Out Insurance" is performed, which loops back to the merge point, or the process proceeds. Alternatively, after the first decision, "Fill in Post Label" is performed, followed by "Check Insurance." After "Check Insurance," a decision is made, leading to a merge point. From the merge point, depending on the outcome, either "Take Out Insurance" is performed, which loops back to the merge point, or the process proceeds. Finally, the two alternative paths merge into "Prepare Paperwork."

After "Select Kind of Shipment" is completed, a decision is made. Depending on the outcome, either "Request Quotes from Carriers" is performed, followed by "Assign Carrier." After "Assign Carrier," another decision is made, leading to a merge point. From the merge point, depending on the outcome, either "Take Out Insurance" is performed, which loops back to the merge point, or the process proceeds. Alternatively, after the first decision, "Fill in Post Label" is performed, followed by "Check Insurance." After "Check Insurance," a decision is made, leading to a merge point. From the merge point, depending on the outcome, either "Take Out Insurance" is performed, which loops back to the merge point, or the process proceeds. Finally, the two alternative paths merge into "Prepare Paperwork."

Once both "Prepare Paperwork" activities are completed, the process continues with "Add Paperwork," followed by "Move Package to Pickup Area," and then the process ends.
