The process starts. Then, two activities, 'Package Goods' and 'Select Kind of Shipment', are performed in parallel. After 'Package Goods' is completed, the process proceeds to a parallel gateway. After 'Select Kind of Shipment' is completed, a decision is made. If 'Request Quotes' is chosen, the process continues with 'Request Quotes from Carriers', followed by 'Assign Carrier' and 'Prepare Paperwork'. Alternatively, the process skips 'Request Quotes from Carriers' and proceeds directly to 'Fill in Post Label', followed by 'Check Insurance'. Depending on whether insurance is required, the process either proceeds to 'Take out Extra Insurance' or skips this step. After 'Take out Extra Insurance' or if no insurance is required, the process merges back. After 'Prepare Paperwork' and after the insurance check, the process merges back. After both parallel activities are completed, the process continues with 'Add Paperwork' and then 'Move Package to Pickup Area', before it ends.
