The process starts with the creation of a production plan and initiation of a process order. Following this, material requirements planning is executed, and the capacity is evaluated. Next, a proposal is scheduled and released, and the process order is confirmed. The process then splits into two alternative paths. One path involves creating a transfer order. The other path involves issuing goods and subsequently confirming the goods issue. After either the transfer order is created or the goods issue is confirmed, the process merges back together. Then, the goods receipt is registered, the order is settled, and the process ends.
