The process starts with the creation of a production plan and initiation of a process order. Next, material requirements planning is executed, followed by an evaluation of capacity. A schedule proposal is then created and released, and the process order is confirmed. Subsequently, a decision is made whether to create a transfer order or confirm a goods issue. If a transfer order is created, the process proceeds; alternatively, if a goods issue is confirmed, the process also proceeds. Following either of these paths, the goods receipt is registered, and the order is settled, concluding the process.
