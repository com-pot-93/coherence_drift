The process starts with the creation of a production plan and initiation of a process order. Next, material requirements planning (MRP) is executed, followed by an evaluation of capacity. A proposal is then scheduled and released, and the process order is confirmed. Subsequently, a decision is made whether to create a transfer order or perform a goods issue. If a transfer order is created, the process proceeds to a point where it merges with the path following the goods issue. Alternatively, if a goods issue is performed, it is then confirmed. After either creating a transfer order or confirming a goods issue, the process continues with the registration of a goods receipt, followed by settling the order, and then the process ends.
