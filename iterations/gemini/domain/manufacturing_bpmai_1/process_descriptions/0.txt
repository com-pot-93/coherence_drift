The process starts. First, a production plan is created and a process order is initiated. Next, Material Requirements Planning (MRP) is executed. Then, the capacity is evaluated. After that, a proposal is scheduled and released. Subsequently, the process order is confirmed. Following this, either a transfer order is created or a goods issue is performed. The goods issue is then confirmed. After confirmation, a goods receipt is registered. Finally, the order is settled, and the process ends.
