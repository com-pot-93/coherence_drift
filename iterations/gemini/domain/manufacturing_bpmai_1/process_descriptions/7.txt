The process starts with the creation of a production plan and the initiation of a process order. Next, material requirements planning is executed, followed by an evaluation of capacity. A proposal is then scheduled and released, and the process order is confirmed. Subsequently, a decision is made whether to create a transfer order or confirm the issue of goods. If a transfer order is created, the process proceeds; alternatively, if goods are issued, the process continues from that point. After either creating a transfer order or confirming the goods issue, the goods receipt is registered. The order is then settled, and the process concludes.
