The process starts with the creation of a production plan and the initiation of a process order. Next, material requirements planning is executed, followed by an evaluation of capacity. A proposal is then scheduled and released, and the process order is confirmed. Subsequently, a decision is made: either a transfer order is created, or goods are issued. If a transfer order is created, the process proceeds to a point where it merges with the path taken if goods are issued. If goods are issued, the goods issue is confirmed first. Following the merge, the goods receipt is registered, and the order is settled, which concludes the process.
