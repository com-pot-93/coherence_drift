The process starts with the creation of a production plan and the initiation of a process order. Next, material requirements planning is executed, followed by an evaluation of capacity. A proposal is then scheduled and released, and the process order is confirmed. Subsequently, a decision is made. If a transfer order is created, the process proceeds to a merge point. Alternatively, if goods are issued, this is followed by confirmation of the goods issue, and then the process proceeds to the same merge point. After the merge point, goods receipt is registered, and the order is settled, which concludes the process.
