The process starts when a travel request is received. Then, a tour plan is prepared and sent. After receiving a response, a decision is made. If the decision is negative, the process ends. Otherwise, the tour is booked. Subsequently, another decision is made. If the decision is negative, the process loops back to the previous decision point. If the decision is positive, the customer is charged. Next, a decision is made whether to contact the customer or send the bill. After contacting the customer or sending the bill, a booking confirmation is sent, and the process loops back to the first decision point. If the first decision is negative, the process ends.
