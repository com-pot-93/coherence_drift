The process starts. A travel request is received, and a tour plan is prepared. The tour plan is then sent, and a response is received. Based on the response, a decision is made. If the response is true, the tour is booked, and another decision is made. If the decision is negative, the process loops back to the first decision point. Otherwise, the customer is charged, and another decision is made between contacting the customer or sending a bill. After either contacting the customer or sending the bill, a booking confirmation is sent, and the process loops back to the first decision point. If the response to the first decision is not true, the process ends.
