The process starts when a travel request is received. Then, a tour plan is prepared and sent. After receiving a response, a decision is made. If the condition is true, the tour is booked, otherwise the process ends. After the tour is booked, another decision is made. If the outcome is negative, the process loops back to the first decision. Otherwise, the customer is charged, and another decision is made to either contact the customer or send a bill. Finally, a booking confirmation is sent, and the process loops back to the first decision.
