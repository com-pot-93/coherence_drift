The process starts with the receipt of a travel request. Subsequently, a tour plan is prepared and sent. After receiving a response, a decision is made. If the condition is true, the tour is booked, otherwise the process ends. After the tour is booked, another decision is made, leading to a negative outcome or the customer being charged. If the customer is charged, another decision is made, leading to either contacting the customer or sending a bill. After contacting the customer or sending the bill, a booking confirmation is sent. After sending the booking confirmation or if the outcome of the second decision was negative, the process loops back to the first decision.
