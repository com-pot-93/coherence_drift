The process starts when a travel request is received. Then, a tour plan is prepared and sent. After the tour plan is sent, a decision is made. If the condition is true, a response is received. After receiving the response, another decision is made, followed by another decision. If the second decision is true, the tour is booked. After booking the tour, another decision is made. If the outcome is negative, the process returns to the "Receive Response" task. Otherwise, the customer is charged. After charging the customer, another decision is made. Depending on the outcome, either the customer is contacted or a bill is sent. After either contacting the customer or sending the bill, a booking confirmation is sent. After sending the booking confirmation, another decision is made, followed by another decision. After the fourth decision, an escalation event occurs, and the process returns to the point before the tour is booked. After the escalation event, the process returns to the first decision point. If the condition is false at the first decision point, the process ends.
