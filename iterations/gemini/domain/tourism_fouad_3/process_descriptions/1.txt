The process starts when a travel request is received. Then, a tour plan is prepared and sent. After receiving a response, a decision is made. If the response is positive, the tour is booked, otherwise the process ends. After the tour is booked, another decision is made. If the outcome is negative, the process returns to the previous decision point. Otherwise, the customer is charged, and another decision is made whether to contact the customer or send a bill. Depending on the decision, either the customer is contacted or a bill is sent. After either of these actions, a booking confirmation is sent, and the process returns to the first decision point.
