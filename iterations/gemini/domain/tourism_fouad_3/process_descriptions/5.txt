The process starts when a travel request is received. Then, a tour plan is prepared and sent. After receiving a response, a decision is made. If the condition is true, the tour is booked. Subsequently, another decision is made. If the outcome is negative, the process loops back to the previous decision point. Otherwise, the customer is charged, and another decision is made to either contact the customer or send a bill. After contacting the customer or sending the bill, a booking confirmation is sent. This leads back to the second decision point, creating a loop. If the condition at the initial decision point is not true, the process ends.
