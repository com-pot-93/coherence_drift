The process starts. First, a travel request is received. Then, a tour plan is prepared and sent. After receiving a response, a decision is made. If the response is positive, the tour is booked, and another decision is made. If the second decision is positive, the customer is charged, and a third decision is made. Depending on the third decision, either the customer is contacted, or the bill is sent, followed by sending the booking confirmation. After contacting the customer or sending the booking confirmation, the process returns to the second decision. If the second decision is negative, the process proceeds to the first decision. If the first decision is negative, the process ends.
