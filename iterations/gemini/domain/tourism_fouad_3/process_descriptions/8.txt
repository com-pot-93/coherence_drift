The process starts when a travel request is received. Then, a tour plan is prepared and sent. After receiving a response, a decision is made. If the response is true, the tour is booked, otherwise the process ends. After the tour is booked, another decision is made. If the decision is negative, the process returns to the previous decision point. Otherwise, the customer is charged, and another decision is made to either contact the customer or send a bill. After contacting the customer or sending the bill, a booking confirmation is sent, and the process returns to the first decision point. The process ends if the first decision is false after receiving a response.
