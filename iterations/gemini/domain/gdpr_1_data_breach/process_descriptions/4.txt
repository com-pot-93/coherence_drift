The process starts. First, breached data is retrieved, followed by retrieving breached subjects. Then, the process splits into two parallel activities. In the first branch, data loss limitation is performed, and the stolen data is evaluated. Depending on whether Condition A is met, a notification is sent, or, if not, the security standard is evaluated. If Condition B is met after evaluating the security standard, a notification is sent. Otherwise, if Condition B is not met, a data subject communication is requested, and a notification for the request is received. After sending a notification or receiving a notification for the request, the process merges back. After that, a notification is sent. In the second parallel branch, the national authority is notified. After both parallel branches are completed, the process merges, and a notification is sent. The process ends.
