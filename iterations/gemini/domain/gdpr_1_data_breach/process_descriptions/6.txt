The process starts. First, breached data is retrieved, followed by retrieving the breached subjects. Then, the process splits into two parallel activities. In the first branch, data loss limitation is performed, and the stolen data is evaluated. Depending on whether Condition A is met, a notification is sent. Subsequently, the process merges via two exclusive gateways. If Condition A is not met, the security standard is evaluated. If Condition B is met, a notification is sent. Otherwise, a data subject communication is requested, and a notification for the request is received. The process merges via two exclusive gateways. In the second parallel branch, the national authority is notified. After both parallel branches are completed, a notification is sent, and the process ends.
