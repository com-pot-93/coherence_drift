The process starts. First, breached data is retrieved, followed by the retrieval of breached subjects. Then, the process splits into two parallel activities. In the first branch, data loss limitation is performed, and the stolen data is evaluated. Depending on whether Condition A is met, a notification is sent; otherwise, the security standard is evaluated. If Condition B is met, the process proceeds; otherwise, a data subject communication is requested, and then the process proceeds. After this, a notification is sent. In the second parallel branch, the national authority is notified. After both parallel branches are completed, a notification is sent, and the process ends.
