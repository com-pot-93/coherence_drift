The process begins with a start event.
Then, the "Retrieve Breached Data" task is performed, followed by the "Retrieve Breached Subjects" task.
After that, the process splits into two parallel paths.
In the first path, the "Data Loss Limitation" task is executed, followed by the "Evaluate Stolen Data" task.
Depending on whether Condition A is met, the process either executes the "Send Notification" task or the "Evaluate Security Standard" task.
After either of these tasks, the process continues to an exclusive gateway.
Depending on whether Condition B is met, the process either proceeds directly to an exclusive gateway or executes the "Request Data Subject Communication" task before reaching the same gateway.
After this gateway, the "Send Notification" task is performed.
In the second parallel path, the "Notify National Authority" task is executed.
After both parallel paths are completed, the process merges back together.
Then, the "Send Notification" task is performed, and the process ends with an end event.
