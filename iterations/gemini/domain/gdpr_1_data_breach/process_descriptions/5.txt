The process starts. First, breached data is retrieved, followed by retrieving the breached subjects. After this, the process splits into two parallel activities. In the first branch, data loss limitation is performed, and then the stolen data is evaluated. Based on whether Condition A is met, the process either sends a notification or evaluates the security standard. If the security standard is evaluated and Condition B is met, a notification is sent. Otherwise, a request for data subject communication is made, and a notification for the request is received. After either sending a notification directly or after the request and notification, the process merges back. In the second parallel branch, the national authority is notified. After both parallel branches are completed, a notification is sent, and the process ends.
