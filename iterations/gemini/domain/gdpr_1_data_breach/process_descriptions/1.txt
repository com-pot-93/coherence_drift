The process starts. First, the breached data is retrieved, followed by retrieving the breached subjects. After this, the process splits into two parallel paths.

In the first path, data loss limitation is performed.

In the second path, the national authority is notified, and then the stolen data is evaluated. Depending on whether Condition A is met, a notification is sent; otherwise, the security standard is evaluated. Depending on whether Condition B is met, a notification is sent; otherwise, a request for data subject communication is made. After that, a notification for the request is received. Depending on whether Condition C is met, a notification is sent; otherwise, no notification is sent.

The second path merges, and the process joins the first path. Finally, the process ends.
