The process starts. First, the breached data is retrieved, followed by retrieving the breached subjects. After this, two activities happen in parallel: "Data loss limitation" and "Notify national authority". After the notification, the stolen data is evaluated. Subsequently, there is a decision point. If the condition is met, a notification is sent. Otherwise, the security standard is evaluated, leading to another decision point. If the condition is met, a notification is sent. Otherwise, a request is made to determine if the breached data needs to be communicated to the data subject, and the notification for the request is received. Then, there is another decision point. If the condition is met, a notification is sent. Otherwise, the process proceeds without sending a notification. Finally, all parallel activities are joined, and the process ends.
