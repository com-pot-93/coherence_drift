The process starts. First, the breached data is retrieved, followed by retrieving the breached subjects. After this, the process splits into two parallel activities.
In the first branch, data loss limitation is performed, and then the stolen data is evaluated. Depending on whether Condition A is met, a notification is sent; otherwise, the security standard is evaluated. If Condition B is met, a notification is sent. Otherwise, a data subject communication is requested, and a notification for the request is received. After sending a notification or receiving a notification for the request, the process continues to the next decision point. Depending on whether Condition C is met, a notification is sent.
In the second parallel branch, the national authority is notified.
Finally, after both parallel branches are completed, the process ends.
