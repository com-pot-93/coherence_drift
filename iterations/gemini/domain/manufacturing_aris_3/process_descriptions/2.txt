The process starts when a production order is received. Then, materials are prepared, and the door is assembled. After the door is assembled, a decision is made. If the condition is true, an inquiry for a quality check is sent and subsequently received. The quality is then checked, and a quality report is sent and received. Another decision is made based on the quality report. If the door needs fixing, it is fixed, and the process returns to the previous decision point. Alternatively, if the door is released, it is released, and the process returns to the previous decision point. If the initial condition is false, the process ends.
