The process starts with the initiation of the process. Then, materials are prepared, and the door is assembled. Following this, a decision is made: if the door needs fixing, an inquiry for a quality check is sent and received. The quality is then checked, and a quality report is sent and received. Another decision is made: if the door still needs fixing, the process loops back to the quality check inquiry. If the door does not need fixing after the quality report, a further decision is made between two paths: if the door needs fixing, it is fixed and then a decision is made to either release the door if it is released or fix the door if it needs fixing. If the door does not need fixing, a decision is made to release the door. Finally, a decision is made to either loop back to the quality check inquiry if the door needs fixing or proceed to the end of the process if the door does not need fixing. If the door does not need fixing after the initial assembly, the process proceeds directly to the end. The process concludes with the end of the process.
