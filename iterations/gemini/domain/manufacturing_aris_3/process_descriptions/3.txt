The process starts. First, materials are prepared, and then the door is assembled. After the door is assembled, a decision is made. If the door needs fixing, an inquiry for a quality check is sent and received. Then, the quality is checked, and a quality report is sent and received. Another decision is made. If the door needs fixing, a third decision is made. If the door needs fixing, the door is fixed, and the process continues to the closing gateway. If the door is released, the door is released, and the process continues to the closing gateway. The closing gateway leads back to the second decision point. If the door does not need fixing after the quality check, the process continues to the closing gateway. If the door does not need fixing after the initial assembly, the process continues to the end. The process ends.
