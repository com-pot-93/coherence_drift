The process starts. First, materials are prepared, and then the door is assembled. After assembling the door, a decision is made. If the decision is true, a quality check inquiry is sent and subsequently received. Then, the quality is checked, and a quality report is sent and received. If the decision is false, the door is fixed. After fixing the door or receiving the quality report, another decision is made. If this decision is true, another decision is made. If this second decision is true, another decision is made. If this third decision is true, the door is fixed again. If the third decision is false or after fixing the door again, another decision is made. If this fourth decision is false, the process continues. If the fourth decision is true, the process is escalated. After escalating or if the fourth decision is false, the process loops back to the second decision. If the second decision is false, the process ends.
