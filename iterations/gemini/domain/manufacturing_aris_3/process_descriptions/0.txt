The process starts when a production order is received. Then, materials are prepared, and the door is assembled. After the door is assembled, a decision is made. If the condition is true, an inquiry for a quality check is sent and received. The quality is then checked, and a quality report is sent and received. Subsequently, another decision is made: either the door is fixed or the door is released. After either fixing or releasing the door, the process loops back to the first decision point. If the initial condition is not true, the process ends.
