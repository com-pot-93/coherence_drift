From here, either "Prescribe NSAID" is chosen, or nothing happens. If "Prescribe NSAID" is chosen, another decision is made, where either "Prescribe opioid" is chosen, or nothing happens. Both paths then merge. The paths where nothing happens also merge.

Finally, all paths merge into a single flow that leads to the "end event," concluding the process.
