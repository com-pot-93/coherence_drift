The process starts. It then enters a loop. If the condition is true, the process continues within the loop; otherwise, it exits the loop. Inside the loop, the following activities are performed in sequence: Production Planning, Assign Workers, Production Design, Product Delivery, Production Logout, and Quality Control. After Quality Control, the process returns to the beginning of the loop. When the process exits the loop, it splits into two parallel paths. One path involves registering to the finished stock and then stocking to the finished goods. The other path involves registering to the semi-finished stock and then loading to the semi-finished stock. After both paths are completed, they merge back together, and the process ends.
