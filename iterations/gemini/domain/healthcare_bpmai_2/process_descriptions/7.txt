The process starts. First, a visit to the patient is conducted, followed by checking the patient's insurance. Subsequently, a decision is made: if the patient is registered, the process proceeds; otherwise, the registration status is checked. If the patient is not registered, they are registered, and a fee is paid. If the patient is already registered, the process proceeds. After registration, an appointment is booked, and the patient undergoes a doctor's examination. Another decision is made: if the patient needs hospitalization, the process proceeds; otherwise, the patient is sent to the pharmacy to order and pay for medicine, after which they receive the medicine. Following this, an appointment is booked, and the patient undergoes another doctor's examination. The process then ends.
