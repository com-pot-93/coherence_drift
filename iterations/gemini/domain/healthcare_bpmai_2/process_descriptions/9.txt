The process starts. First, a visit is conducted, and then the insurance is checked. If the patient is registered, an appointment is booked, and after that, the process continues based on some unspecified criteria, eventually merging back. If the patient is not registered, it is checked whether they are already registered. If they are already registered, an appointment is booked, and the process continues based on some unspecified criteria, eventually merging back. If the patient is not already registered, they are registered, a fee is paid, and the process merges back. After the merge, a doctor examination is performed. If hospitalization is needed, the process proceeds to a merge point. If no hospitalization is needed, medicine is ordered and paid for, then received, and the process proceeds to the same merge point. After the merge, another appointment is booked, followed by another doctor examination, and the process ends.
