The process starts with a nurse visiting a patient. Subsequently, the insurance is checked. Depending on the outcome of the insurance check, the process either proceeds directly to booking an appointment if the patient is already registered, or it first checks the patient's registration status. If the patient is not registered, they are registered and then required to pay a fee. After paying the fee, or if the patient was already registered, the process continues to book an appointment. Following the appointment booking, the patient undergoes a doctor's examination. Based on the examination results, the patient is either hospitalized, sent to the pharmacy to order and pay for medicine and then receive it, or sent home. After any of these three paths, another appointment is booked, followed by another doctor's examination, and the process ends.
