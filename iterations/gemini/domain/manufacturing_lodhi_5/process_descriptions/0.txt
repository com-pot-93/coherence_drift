The process starts when a production order is received. The production order is then prepared. Depending on certain conditions, the process either proceeds to collect raw materials or ends. If raw materials are collected, they are then cut. After cutting, the process splits into two parallel activities: stitching and printing. Once both stitching and printing are completed, the production is completed. The quality is then checked, and a quality report is created. Depending on the outcome of the quality check, either settings are adjusted or data is sent to the warehouse. After either adjusting settings or sending data to the warehouse, the process loops back to the decision point where raw materials are collected or the process ends. If the process does not collect raw materials, it ends.
