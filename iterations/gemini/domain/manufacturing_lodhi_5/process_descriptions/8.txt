The process starts with the initiation of production order preparation. Following this, a decision is made. If the condition is true, raw materials are collected and then cut. After cutting, the process splits into two parallel activities: stitching and printing. Once both stitching and printing are completed, the production is finalized, followed by a quality check. A quality report is then created. Another decision is made: either settings are adjusted or data is sent to the warehouse. After either adjusting settings or sending data to the warehouse, the process loops back to the initial decision point. If the initial condition is false, the process ends.
