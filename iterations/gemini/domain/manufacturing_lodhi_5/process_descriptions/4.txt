The process starts with the initiation of production order preparation. Following this, a decision is made. If the condition is true, raw materials are collected and then cut. After cutting, the process splits into two parallel activities: stitching and printing. Once both are completed, the process merges and proceeds to finalize production. A quality check is then performed, and a quality report is created. Another decision is made: either settings are adjusted or data is sent to the warehouse. After either of these actions, the process loops back to the first decision point. If the initial condition is not true, the process ends.
