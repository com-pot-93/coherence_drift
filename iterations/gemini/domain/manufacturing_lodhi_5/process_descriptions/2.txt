The process starts with the initiation of production order preparation. Following this, a decision is made. If the condition is true, raw materials are collected and then cut. Subsequently, two parallel activities occur simultaneously: stitching and printing. Once both are completed, the production is finalized, and a quality check is performed. A quality report is then created, after which another decision is made. Depending on the outcome, either settings are adjusted or data is sent to the warehouse. Finally, the process loops back to the initial decision point, and if the condition is not true, the process ends.
