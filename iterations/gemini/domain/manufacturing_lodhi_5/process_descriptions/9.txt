The process starts with the initiation of production order preparation. Subsequently, a decision is made. If the condition is true, raw materials are collected and then cut. Following this, the process splits into two parallel activities: stitching and printing. Once both are completed, the production is finalized, and a quality check is performed. A quality report is then created. Another decision is made: either settings are adjusted, or data is sent to the warehouse. After either of these actions, the process loops back to the initial decision point. If the initial condition is false, the process ends.
