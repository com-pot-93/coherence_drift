The process starts with the initiation of production order preparation. Following this, a decision is made. If the condition is true, raw materials are collected and then cut. After cutting, the process splits into two parallel activities: stitching and printing. Once both are completed, the process continues with finalizing production, followed by a quality check and the creation of a quality report. Another decision is then made, leading to one of two possible paths: either settings are adjusted, or data is sent to the warehouse. After either of these actions, the process loops back to the initial decision point. If the initial condition is not true, the process ends.
