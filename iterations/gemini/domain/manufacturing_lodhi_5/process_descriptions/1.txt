The process starts with the initiation of a production order preparation. After the preparation, a decision is made. If the condition is true, raw materials are collected and then cut. Following the cutting of raw materials, two activities occur in parallel: stitching and printing. Once both stitching and printing are completed, the production is finalized. Subsequently, a quality check is performed, and a quality report is created. Another decision is made: either settings are adjusted, or data is sent to the warehouse. After either adjusting settings or sending data to the warehouse, the process loops back to the initial decision point. If the initial condition is false, the process ends.
