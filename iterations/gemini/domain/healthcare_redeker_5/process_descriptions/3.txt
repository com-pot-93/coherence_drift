The process starts. First, the room is prepared. Then, surgery is performed. After the surgery, two activities occur in parallel: the patient is discharged and the room is sanitized. Once both are completed, a report is filled out. Subsequently, a decision is made. If the bill is paid, the process proceeds to the next step. If the bill is not paid, an alternative step is taken to record that the bill remains unpaid. Finally, the process ends.
