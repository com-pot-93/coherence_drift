The process starts with the start event. Then, the room is prepared, followed by performing the surgery. After the surgery, the process splits into two parallel activities: discharging the patient and sanitizing the room. Once both activities are completed, a report is filled out. Subsequently, a decision is made based on whether the bill has been paid. If the bill is paid, the process proceeds to the next step. If the bill is not paid, the unpaid bill is recorded. Finally, after either of these two paths, the process ends.
