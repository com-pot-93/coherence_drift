The process starts when a shipment request is received. The availability of the product is then checked. If the product is not available, the process proceeds to prepare a service station shipment. Otherwise, if the product is available, the shipment address is checked. If the address is an inbound address, an inbound shipment is assembled and sent, then the inbound shipment is received and checked. If the address is a regional address, the products are prepared for regional shipment. After preparing either the inbound or regional shipment, the process proceeds to prepare a service station shipment. Finally, the process ends after the service station shipment is prepared.
