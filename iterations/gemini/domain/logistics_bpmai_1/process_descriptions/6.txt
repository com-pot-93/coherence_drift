The process starts when a shipment request is received. Then, the availability of the product is checked. If the product is not available, the process proceeds to prepare the service station shipment. Otherwise, if the product is available, the shipment address is checked. Depending on the location, the process follows different paths. If the location is inbound, the inbound shipment is assembled and sent, then the inbound shipment is received and checked, and the process proceeds to prepare the service station shipment. If the location is regional, the products are prepared for regional shipment, and the process proceeds to prepare the service station shipment. After preparing the products for the service station shipment, the service station shipment is prepared, and the process ends.
