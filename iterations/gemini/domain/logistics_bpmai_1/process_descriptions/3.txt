The process starts when a shipment request is received. Then, the availability of the product is checked. If the product is not available, a service station shipment is prepared. Otherwise, if the product is available, the shipment address is checked. If the address is an inbound location, the inbound shipment is assembled and sent, then the inbound shipment is received and checked, and the products are prepared for service station shipment. If the address is a regional location, the products are prepared for regional shipment. Finally, the process ends.
