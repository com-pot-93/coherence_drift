The process starts when a shipment request is received. Then, the availability of the product is checked. If the product is not available, the process proceeds to prepare a service station shipment. Otherwise, if the product is available, the shipment address is checked. If the address is an inbound address, the process assembles and sends an inbound shipment, then receives and checks the inbound shipment, and proceeds to prepare a service station shipment. If the address is a regional address, the process prepares the products for regional shipment and proceeds to prepare a service station shipment. Finally, the service station shipment is prepared, and the process ends.
