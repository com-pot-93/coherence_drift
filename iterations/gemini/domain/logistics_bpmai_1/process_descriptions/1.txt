The process starts when a shipment request is received. The availability of the product is then checked. If the product is available, the shipment address is checked, and depending on the destination, either an inbound shipment is assembled and sent, or products are prepared for regional shipment. If it is an inbound shipment, the inbound shipment is received and checked, and then the products are prepared for shipment to the service station. If the product is not available, the products are prepared for shipment to the service station directly. Finally, the process ends.
