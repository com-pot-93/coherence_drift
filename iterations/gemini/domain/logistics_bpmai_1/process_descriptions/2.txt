The process starts when a shipment request is received. Then, the availability of the product is checked. If the product is not available, preparations are made for a service station shipment. Otherwise, if the product is available, the shipment address is checked. Depending on whether the destination is an inbound location or a regional location, either an inbound shipment is assembled and sent, or products are prepared for regional shipment. In the case of an inbound destination, after the inbound shipment is received and checked, the products are prepared for shipment to a service station. Finally, the process ends.
