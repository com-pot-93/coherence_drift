The process starts when a shipment request is received. Then, the availability of the product is checked. If the product is not available, a service station shipment is prepared. Otherwise, if the product is available, the shipment address is checked. If the shipment address is an inbound location, an inbound shipment is assembled and sent, then the inbound shipment is received and checked, and the products are prepared for service station shipment. If the shipment address is a regional location, the products are prepared for regional shipment. After preparing products for either regional or inbound shipment, a service station shipment is prepared. The process ends after the service station shipment is prepared.
