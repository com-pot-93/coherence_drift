The process starts when a shipment request is received. The availability of the product is then checked. If the product is not available, the process proceeds to prepare a service station shipment. Otherwise, if the product is available, the shipment address is checked. Depending on the address, the process either assembles an inbound shipment and sends it, then receives and checks the inbound shipment, or prepares the products for regional shipment. After either the inbound shipment is checked or the products are prepared for regional shipment, the process proceeds to prepare a service station shipment. Finally, the service station shipment is prepared, and the process ends.
