The process starts with the event of starting the process.
First, the product is packaged.
Then, the packaged product is shipped.
Next, a decision is made.
If a receipt is needed, it is printed and then posted. Otherwise, if no receipt is needed, this step is skipped.
After printing and posting the receipt, or if no receipt is needed, the product is delivered to the station.
In parallel, another decision is made.
If the product is received, it is processed as received.
In parallel with that, another decision is made.
If the product is received, it is processed as received. Otherwise, if the product is not received, this step is skipped.
After processing the product as received or skipping the processing, the process continues.
After processing the product as received, the process continues.
Finally, the process ends.
