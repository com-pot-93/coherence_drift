The process starts. First, the product is packaged, and then it is shipped. Subsequently, a decision is made. If the product is to be delivered to the door, it is delivered, and another decision is made. Depending on the outcome, either a pick-up receipt is printed, then posted, and the product is rerouted to the closest station, or the process proceeds directly to the next decision point.
If the product is to be delivered to the station, it is delivered, and the process continues to another decision point.
From there, depending on the outcome, the process either proceeds directly to the next decision point or the product is received back.
Finally, the process ends.
