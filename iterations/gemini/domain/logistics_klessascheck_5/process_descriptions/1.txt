The process starts. First, the product is packaged, then it is shipped. Subsequently, a decision is made. If the product is to be delivered to the door, it is done so, and another decision is made regarding whether to print a receipt. If a receipt is printed, a pick-up receipt is printed, the receipt is posted, and the product is rerouted to the station. If a receipt is not printed, the process proceeds to a merging point.
Alternatively, if the product is to be delivered to the station, it is done so, and a decision is made about whether the product is received back. If the product is received back, it is received, and the process proceeds to a merging point. If the product is not received, the process also proceeds to the merging point.
Finally, the process merges and ends.
