The process starts when a customer order is received. This order is then processed, and the necessary components are determined based on the existing plan. Following this, a decision is made: if any parts are missing, they are purchased and added to the stock; otherwise, the process continues directly. Next, the production is carried out, followed by quality checks. Another decision is made based on the quality checks: if follow-up production is required, it is performed; otherwise, the process continues directly. The product is then filled into packaging, packed, and labeled. After packing, the product is transported to the warehouse and finally sent to the customer, which concludes the process.
