The process starts when a customer order is received. The order is then processed, and the necessary components are determined. If parts are missing, the missing parts are purchased and then added to the stock. If all parts are available or after the missing parts are added to the stock, the production starts. After the production, quality checks are performed. If a follow-up is required, it is carried out. If no follow-up is required or after the follow-up is carried out, the product is packaged, filled into packaging, packed and labeled, and transported to the warehouse. Finally, the product is sent to the customer, which ends the process.
