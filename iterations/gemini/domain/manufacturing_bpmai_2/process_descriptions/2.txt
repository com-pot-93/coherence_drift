The process starts when a customer order is received. The order is then processed, and the necessary components are determined. If parts are missing, the missing parts are purchased and added to stock. Otherwise, if all parts are available, the process proceeds directly to production. After production, quality checks are performed. If follow-up is required, it is carried out. Otherwise, if no follow-up is required, the process proceeds directly to packaging. The product is then filled into packaging, packed and labeled, and transported to the warehouse. Finally, the product is sent to the customer, and the process ends.
