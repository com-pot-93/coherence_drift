The process starts when an order is received. The order is then processed, and the necessary components are determined. If parts are missing, the missing parts are purchased and added to the stock. Otherwise, if all parts are available or after the missing parts are added to the stock, the production starts. After the production is started, quality checks are performed. If a follow-up is required, it is carried out. Otherwise, or after the follow-up is carried out, the product is packaged, filled into packaging, packed and labeled, and transported to the warehouse. Finally, the product is sent to the customer, and the process ends.
