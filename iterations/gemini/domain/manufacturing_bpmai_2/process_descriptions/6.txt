The process starts when an order is received. The order is then processed, and the necessary components are determined.
If parts are missing, the missing parts are purchased and then added to the stock. If all parts are available, the process skips directly to starting the production.
After production starts, quality checks are performed. If follow-up is required, it is carried out; otherwise, the process proceeds directly.
The product is then packaged, filled into packaging, packed and labeled, transported to the warehouse, and finally sent to the customer, which ends the process.
