The process starts when a customer order is received. The order is then processed, and the necessary components are determined. If parts are missing, they are purchased and then added to stock. If all parts are available or after the missing parts are added to stock, the production is carried out. After production, quality checks are performed. If a follow-up is required, it is carried out. Whether a follow-up is necessary or not, the product is then filled into packaging, packed and labeled, and transported to the warehouse. Finally, the product is sent to the customer, and the process ends.
