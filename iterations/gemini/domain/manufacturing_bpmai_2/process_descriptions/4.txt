The process starts when an order is received. The order is then processed, and the necessary components are determined. If parts are missing, the missing parts are purchased and added to the stock. Otherwise, if all parts are available, the process proceeds directly. After ensuring all parts are available, production starts, followed by quality checks. If a follow-up is required after the quality checks, it is carried out. Otherwise, if no follow-up is required, the process proceeds directly. Subsequently, the product is packaged, filled into packaging, packed and labeled, and transported to the warehouse. Finally, the product is sent to the customer, and the process ends.
