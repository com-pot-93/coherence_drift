The process starts with retrieving temperature values. Based on the temperature values, the process either generates a standard notification or proceeds to gather more information in parallel.

In parallel, the process retrieves the container status and estimates it. Based on the estimation, it either generates a container change request or proceeds without it.

Also in parallel, the process retrieves the client location and estimates the distance. Based on the distance, it either estimates the delivery time and generates a route change request based on the estimation, or proceeds without estimating the delivery time.

In parallel, the process retrieves the goods status and estimates it. Based on the estimation, it either evaluates damages and then either generates a removal request or generates a price change request, or proceeds without evaluating damages.

After gathering all the information in parallel, the process sends the generated messages. Finally, the process either ends after sending the generated messages or after generating a standard notification.
