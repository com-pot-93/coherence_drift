The process starts when a data subject request is received. The relevance of the withdrawn data is then checked. If the data is relevant, the process stops using the withdrawn data. Otherwise, the user is informed about the impact of the withdrawal, and the process waits for a continuation notification. If the notification indicates to stop the processes, the running business processes are stopped. If the notification indicates to continue, the process continues. Finally, the withdrawal is communicated, and the process ends.
