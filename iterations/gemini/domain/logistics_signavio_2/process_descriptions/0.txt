The process starts when a customer order is received. Then, stock items are picked and packed into parcels. The parcels are weighed, and shipping labels are printed. After that, three activities occur in parallel: Parcels are moved to the loading dock, the parcel count is checked, and the packing quality is inspected. Once all these activities are completed, two more activities are performed in parallel: Parcels are loaded on the truck, and delivery instructions are sent to the transporter; at the same time, the order status and stock levels are updated. The process ends when both sets of parallel activities are finished.
