The process starts when an order is received. Then, the items are picked and packed, and the parcels are weighed. After weighing, shipping labels are printed. Next, three activities occur in parallel: Parcels are moved to the loading dock, the parcel count is checked, and the packing quality is inspected. Once all three activities are completed, another set of three activities occur in parallel: Parcels are loaded on the truck, delivery instructions are sent, and the order status and stock levels are updated. Finally, after all three of these activities are completed, the process ends.
