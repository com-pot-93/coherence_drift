The process starts. It then splits into two parallel activities.
In the first branch, a price request is sent to airlines, and the responses from airlines are received. Then, an airline is selected.
In the second branch, a price request is sent to hotels, and the responses from hotels are received. Then, a hotel is selected.
After completing both branches, the process merges back into a single flow. A tour plan is prepared and sent to the customer. The process ends.
