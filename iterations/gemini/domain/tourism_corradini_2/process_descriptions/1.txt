The process starts with the initiation of a start event. Subsequently, the system searches for a customer. After finding the customer, an offer is made. Depending on whether the offer is accepted or rejected, the process follows different paths. If the offer is accepted, the process proceeds directly to an end event. If the offer is rejected, the customer makes a request, and details are provided in response. Following the provision of details, the process merges back with the path where the offer was accepted, and the process concludes with an end event.
