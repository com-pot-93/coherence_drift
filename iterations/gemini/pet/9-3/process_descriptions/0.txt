The process begins with the Coordination Unit drafting a schedule of meetings. Subsequently, the Coordination Unit adds these draft dates to all calendars. Following this, a Support Officer checks the dates and then suggests any necessary modifications. The Coordination Unit then rechecks all the dates and looks for potential conflicts. After this review, the Coordination Unit sends the final schedule to the Committee Members, who then check their diaries. Finally, the Committee Members advise the Coordination Unit of any conflicts, which concludes the process.
