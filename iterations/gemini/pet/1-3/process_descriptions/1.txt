The process starts when an order is taken. The order ticket is then submitted, and the order is given to the sommelier. After that, the order is assigned to a waiter. Following this, three activities occur in parallel: the kitchen prepares the food, the sommelier selects the drinks, and the waiter readies the cart. Once all three are completed, the cart is delivered, and the waiter returns to the station. At this point, a decision is made. Either the process waits for billing, or the guest account is debited. Finally, the process ends.
