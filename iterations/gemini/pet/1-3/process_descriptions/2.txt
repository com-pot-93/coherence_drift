The process starts when an order is taken. Subsequently, the order ticket is submitted, and the order is given to the sommelier. The order is then assigned to a waiter. Following this, three activities occur in parallel: food preparation, drink selection, and cart preparation. Once all three are completed, the cart is delivered and then returned to the station. At this point, a decision is made. Either the process waits for billing, or the guest account is debited. Finally, after either waiting for billing or debiting the guest account, the process ends.
