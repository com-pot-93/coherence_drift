The process starts. First, an order is taken, and a ticket is submitted. Then, the order is given to the sommelier, who assigns it to a waiter. After that, food preparation, drink selection, and cart preparation occur in parallel. Once all three are complete, the cart is delivered and then returned to the station. Next, there is a decision based on whether billing is needed. If billing is needed, the process waits for billing and proceeds. If not, the process skips waiting. Finally, the guest account is debited, and the process ends.
