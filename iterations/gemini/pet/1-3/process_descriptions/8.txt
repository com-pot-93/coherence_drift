The process starts when an order is taken and a ticket is submitted. Subsequently, the order is assigned to a waiter. Following this, three activities occur in parallel: food preparation, drink selection, and cart preparation. Once all three are completed, the cart is delivered. The cart is then returned to the station. Next, a decision is made: if billing is needed, the process waits for billing; otherwise, this step is skipped. Finally, the guest account is debited, and the process ends.
