The process starts when an order is taken. Then, a ticket is submitted, and the order is given to the sommelier. After that, the order is assigned to a waiter. Following this, food preparation, drink selection, and cart preparation occur in parallel. Once all these tasks are completed, the cart is delivered, and then the cart is returned to the station. Next, there is a decision point. If the process needs to wait for billing, it waits; otherwise, the guest account is debited. Finally, the process ends.
