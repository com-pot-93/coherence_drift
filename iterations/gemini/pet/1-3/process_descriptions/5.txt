The process starts when an order is taken. Subsequently, a ticket is submitted, and the order is given to the sommelier. The order is then assigned to a waiter. Following this, food preparation, drink selection, and cart preparation occur in parallel. After all three are completed, the cart is delivered, and then returned to the station. Next, a decision is made: if billing is needed, the process waits for billing; otherwise, this step is skipped. Finally, the guest account is debited, and the process ends.
