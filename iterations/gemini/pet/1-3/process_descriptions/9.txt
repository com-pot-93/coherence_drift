The process starts. An order is taken and a ticket is submitted. The order is then assigned to a waiter. Following this, three activities occur simultaneously: food preparation, drink selection, and cart preparation. Once all three are completed, the cart is delivered. The cart is then returned to the station. A decision is made: if billing is required, the process waits for billing; otherwise, this step is skipped. Finally, the guest account is debited, and the process ends.
