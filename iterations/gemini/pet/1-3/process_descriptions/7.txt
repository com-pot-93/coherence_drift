The process starts. An order is taken and a ticket is submitted. The order is then assigned to a waiter. Following this, three activities occur in parallel: food preparation, drink selection, and cart preparation. Once all three are completed, the cart is delivered. The cart is then returned to the station. At this point, a decision is made. If billing is needed, the process waits for billing. Otherwise, if no billing is needed, the process proceeds directly. After the billing is completed or skipped, the guest account is debited, and the process ends.
