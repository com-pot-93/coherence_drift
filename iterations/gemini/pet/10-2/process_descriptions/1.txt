The process starts with the initiation of a "Report Meter Operation" task. Following this, an "Examine Application" task is performed. The process then diverges based on a decision point. If the application is rejected, a "Reject Application" task is executed. Alternatively, if the application is confirmed, a "Confirm Application" task is performed. Regardless of whether the application was rejected or confirmed, the process merges and proceeds to an "Inform MPOO and MSPO" task. Next, a "Perform Equipment Acquisition/Changes" task is carried out, leading to another decision point. If the outcome is a failure, an "Inform GO about Failure" task is executed, followed by another decision point. Here, the process either executes an "Inform MPON about Failure" task or a "Confirm Failure of Assignment" task. After either of these tasks, the process merges. If the outcome of the "Perform Equipment Acquisition/Changes" task is a success, an "Inform GO about Completion" task is performed, followed by an "Assign MPON and Confirm" task. The process merges again and proceeds to an "Inform MPOO, MSPO, and SP" task, which leads to the end of the process.
