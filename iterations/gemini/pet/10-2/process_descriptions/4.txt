The process starts with the initiation of a "Report Meter Operation" task. Following this, an "Examine Application" task is performed. The process then diverges based on the outcome of the examination. If the application is "Rejected," a "Reject Application" task is executed. Alternatively, if the application is "Confirmed," a "Confirm Application" task is performed. Regardless of whether the application was rejected or confirmed, the process converges to an "Inform MPOO and MSPO" task. Next, a "Perform Equipment Acquisition/Changes" task is carried out. The process then branches again depending on whether the equipment acquisition/changes were successful. If "Success," an "Inform GO about Completion" task is executed, followed by an "Assign MPON and Confirm" task. If there was a failure, an "Inform GO about Failure" task is performed, leading to another branching point. If the decision is to "Inform MPON," then an "Inform MPON about Failure" task is performed. If the decision is to "Confirm Failure," a "Confirm Failure of Assignment" task is executed. Whether the MPON is informed or the failure is confirmed, the process converges. After informing GO about completion or after the failure-related tasks, the process merges again and an "Inform MPOO, MSPO, and SP" task is performed. The process concludes with an end event.
