The process starts with the creation of a report on meter operation. This report is then examined. Based on the examination, the report is either rejected or confirmed. If rejected, the application is rejected. If confirmed, the application is confirmed. After either rejection or confirmation, MPOO and MSPO are informed. Next, equipment acquisition or changes are performed. If this is successful, GO is informed about the completion. If it fails, GO is informed about the failure. In case of failure, there are two possible paths: either MPON is informed about the failure, or the failure of the assignment is confirmed. After informing MPON or confirming the failure, the process merges back. Subsequently, after informing GO about completion or after the merge following a failure, MPON is assigned and confirmed. Finally, MPOO, MSPO, and SP are informed, and the process ends.
