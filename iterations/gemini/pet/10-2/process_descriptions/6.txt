The process starts with the initiation event.
First, a report on meter operation is created.
Next, the application is examined.
Then, a decision is made: if the application is rejected, it is rejected; otherwise, if the application is confirmed, it is confirmed.
After either rejection or confirmation, MPOO and MSPO are informed.
Following this, equipment acquisition or changes are performed.
Another decision is made: if the acquisition/changes are successful, GO is informed about the completion, and then MPON is assigned and confirmed; otherwise, if the acquisition/changes fail, GO is informed about the failure.
If informing GO about the failure occurs, another decision is made: either MPON is informed about the failure, or the failure of the assignment is confirmed.
After either informing MPON or confirming the failure, the process merges back.
After assigning and confirming MPON, or after the process merges back following a failure, MPOO, MSPO, and SP are informed.
Finally, the process ends.
