The process starts with the initiation of a "Report Meter Operation." Following this, an "Examine Application" task is performed. The process then diverges based on the outcome of the examination. If the application is "Rejected," a "Reject Application" task is executed. Alternatively, if the application is "Confirmed," a "Confirm Application" task is performed. Regardless of whether the application was rejected or confirmed, the process converges to an "Inform MPOO and MSPO" task. Next, "Perform Equipment Acquisition/Changes" is carried out, leading to another decision point. If the acquisition/changes are "Success"ful, the process proceeds to "Inform GO about Completion" and subsequently "Assign MPON and Confirm." However, if the acquisition/changes "Failure," the process executes "Inform GO about Failure," which leads to another decision. Depending on whether to "Inform MPON" or "Confirm Failure," either "Inform MPON about Failure" or "Confirm Failure of Assignment" is performed. After either informing MPON about the failure or confirming the failure of assignment, the process merges and continues to a common task: "Inform MPOO, MSPO, and SP." The process concludes with an end event.
