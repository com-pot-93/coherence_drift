The process starts with the creation of a report on meter operation. This report is then examined, leading to a decision point. If the application is rejected, the process proceeds to reject the application. Alternatively, if the application is confirmed, the process confirms the application. Regardless of whether the application was rejected or confirmed, the next step is to inform both MPOO and MSPO. Following this, equipment acquisition or changes are performed. Another decision point is reached: if the acquisition or changes are successful, the GO is informed about the completion, and then the MPON is assigned and confirmed. If the acquisition or changes are not successful, the GO is informed about the failure. This leads to another decision point: either the MPON is informed about the failure, or the failure of the assignment is confirmed. Whether the MPON is informed about the failure or the failure of assignment is confirmed, the process then proceeds to the assignment and confirmation of the MPON. After the MPON is assigned and confirmed, MPOO, MSPO, and SP are informed. The process then ends.
