The process starts with the initiation of a "Report Meter Operation." Subsequently, an "Examine Application" task is performed. Following this, a decision is made: if the application is rejected, a "Reject Application" task is executed; otherwise, if the application is confirmed, a "Confirm Application" task is performed. After either rejection or confirmation, the process proceeds to "Inform MPOO and MSPO." Next, "Perform Equipment Acquisition/Changes" is carried out, leading to another decision point. If the outcome is a failure, "Inform GO about Failure" is performed, followed by another decision: either "Inform MPON about Failure" or "Confirm Failure of Assignment." If the outcome of the "Perform Equipment Acquisition/Changes" task is a success, "Inform GO about Completion" is performed, followed by "Assign MPON and Confirm." After either informing about failure or assigning and confirming, the process continues to "Inform MPOO, MSPO, and SP," and then the process ends.
