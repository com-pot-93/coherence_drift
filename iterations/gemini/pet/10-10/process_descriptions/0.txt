The process starts when an event occurs. Then, a decision is made. If a fault detector reports a failure to the MPO, the MPO examines the failure. Otherwise, if the MPO suspects its own fault, the MPO examines the failure.
After the MPO examines the failure, a decision is made. If the MPO rejects the failure of the fault detector, the process ends. Otherwise, if the MPO confirms the failure of the fault detector, the MPO informs the GO, and then informs the MSP. Next, the MPO fixes the fault at the measuring device, shares the results of the repairs, informs the GO about the resolution of the interference and informs the MSP about the resolution of the interference. Finally, the process ends.
