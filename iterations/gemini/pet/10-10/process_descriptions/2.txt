The process starts with the initiation of an event. Subsequently, a task is performed to examine a failure. Following this examination, a decision is made. If the failure is rejected, the process proceeds to an end event. Alternatively, if the failure is confirmed, the process continues with a task to inform a group (GO). Next, another group (MSP) is informed. After informing both groups, a task is executed to fix the fault. Following the fault fix, the repair results are shared. Subsequently, the GO is informed about the resolution, and then the MSP is informed about the resolution. Finally, the process merges back with the rejected branch and concludes with an end event.
