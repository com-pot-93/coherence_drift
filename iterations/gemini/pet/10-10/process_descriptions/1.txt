The process starts when the MPO examines a failure. Based on the examination, if the MPO rejects the failure of the fault detector, the process ends. Alternatively, if the MPO confirms the failure, they inform the GO, then inform the MSP, and proceed to fix the fault at the measuring device. After fixing the fault, the MPO shares the results of the repairs, informs the GO about the resolution of the interference, and informs the MSP about the resolution of the interference. Finally, the process ends.
