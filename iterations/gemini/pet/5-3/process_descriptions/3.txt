The process starts when an employee submits an office supply request. This request is then registered. Depending on whether the request is approved or not, the process follows different paths. If the request is not approved, it is returned to the employee, who reviews the comments and resubmits it. The process then merges back to a single path. If the request is approved, it is received for approval, and quotations are requested. The process then waits until a vendor is selected. If a vendor is not selected, a different vendor is chosen, and the process returns to the step where the process waits until a vendor is selected. Once a vendor is selected, a purchase order is generated, and the process waits for the product delivery. After the product is delivered, an invoice is received, and a notification is sent to the user, which concludes the process.
