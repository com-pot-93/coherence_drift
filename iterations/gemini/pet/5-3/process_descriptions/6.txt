The process starts with the submission of an office supply request. This request is then registered. Depending on a condition, the process either proceeds to an approval process or terminates. If the approval process is initiated, it leads to a decision point. If the request is not approved, it is returned to the employee, who reviews the comments and resubmits the request for another approval process. If the request is approved, the process moves to request quotations. After quotations are requested, the process waits for vendor selection, then generates a purchase order, and waits for product delivery. Once the product is delivered, an invoice is received, and a notification is sent to the user. Following the notification, there is a decision point. If the condition is met, the process loops back to request quotations. Otherwise, an escalation event is triggered, which leads back to the initial decision point.
