The process starts when an employee submits an office supply request. This request is then registered. The immediate supervisor receives the request and approves it. Depending on the approval decision, one of the following paths is taken. If the request is not approved, the immediate supervisor returns the request to the employee, who reviews the comments. Otherwise, if the request is approved, or after the employee reviews the comments, the process proceeds to the next step.
The request then goes to the purchase department, which makes quotations and selects a vendor. Depending on the vendor selection, one of two paths is taken. If the purchase department does not choose the selected vendor, it chooses a different vendor. Otherwise, if the purchase department confirms the selected vendor, or after the purchase department chooses a different vendor, the system generates a purchase order and sends it. The system then waits for the product delivery and receives the invoice.
Finally, the system sends a user notification, and the process ends.
