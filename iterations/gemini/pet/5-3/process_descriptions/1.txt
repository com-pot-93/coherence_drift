The process starts when an employee submits an office supply request. This request is then registered and received for approval. If the request is not approved, it is returned to the employee, who reviews the comments and resubmits the request for approval. If the request is approved, the process proceeds to the purchase department. Here, quotations are made, and a vendor is selected. If a vendor is not selected, a different vendor is chosen, and the selection process is repeated. Once a vendor is selected, a purchase order is generated and sent. The process then waits for the product delivery and receives the invoice. Finally, a notification is sent to the user, and the process ends.
