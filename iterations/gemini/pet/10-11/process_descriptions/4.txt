The process starts with a request for measurements. This request is then checked. Depending on the outcome of the check, the request is either denied or the measurement is performed. If the request is denied, the process proceeds to a point where it merges with another path. If the measurement is performed, the process continues, but the path splits based on whether the measurement was successful or failed. If the measurement failed, the GO is informed, and the process merges with another path. If the measurement was successful, the measured values are transmitted, processed, and the changed values are sent. This path then merges with the path of the failed measurement. After this merge, the process merges with the path where the request was denied. Finally, the readings are transmitted to the SP, and the process ends.
