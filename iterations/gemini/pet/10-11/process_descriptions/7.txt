The process starts with a request being checked. Depending on the outcome, the request is either denied or a measurement is performed. If the request is denied, the process proceeds to a merging point. If a measurement is performed, its outcome is evaluated. If the measurement fails, a notification is sent. If the measurement is successful, the measured values are transmitted, then processed, and the changed values are sent. Regardless of whether the measurement failed or the changed values were sent, the process proceeds to a merging point. After either denying the request or processing a successful or failed measurement, the readings are transmitted, and the process ends.
