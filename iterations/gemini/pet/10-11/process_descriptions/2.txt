The process starts with a request for measurements. This request is then checked. Depending on the outcome of the check, the request is either denied or the measurement is performed. If the request is denied, the process proceeds to a merge point. If the measurement is performed, the process continues, but the next step depends on whether the measurement was successful or a failure. In case of failure, the GO is informed, and the process proceeds to a merge point. In case of success, the measured values are transmitted, then processed, and the changed values are sent. Subsequently, the readings are transmitted to the SP, and the process proceeds to a merge point. After this merge point, the process arrives at another merge point before it ends.
