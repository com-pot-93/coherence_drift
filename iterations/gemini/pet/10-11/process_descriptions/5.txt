The process starts with a request for measurements. This request is then checked. Depending on the outcome of the check, the process either proceeds to deny the request or to perform the measurement. If the request is denied, the process continues to a merging point. If the measurement is performed, the process continues depending on whether the measurement was successful or failed. If the measurement failed, the GO is informed, and the process continues to a merging point. If the measurement was successful, the measured values are transmitted and then processed. The changed values are then sent, and the process continues to a merging point. After this merging point, the process continues to another merging point. From this merging point, the readings are transmitted to the SP, and the process ends.
