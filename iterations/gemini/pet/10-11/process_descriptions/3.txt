The process starts with a request for measurements. This request is then checked. Depending on the outcome of the check, the process either proceeds to deny the request or to perform the measurement. If the request is denied, the process continues to a merge gateway. If the measurement is performed, the process continues to another decision point. If the measurement fails, the GO is informed, and the process continues to a merge gateway. If the measurement is successful, the measured values are transmitted, then processed, and the changed values are sent. Subsequently, the readings are transmitted to the SP, and the process continues to a merge gateway. After this merge gateway, the process continues to another merge gateway, and finally, the process ends.
