The process starts when a request for measurements is initiated. The request is then checked. Depending on the outcome of the check, the process either proceeds to deny the request or to perform the measurement. If the request is denied, the process continues to a gateway. Alternatively, if the measurement is performed, the process continues depending on whether the measurement was successful or failed. If the measurement failed, the GO is informed, and the process continues to a gateway. If the measurement was successful, the measured values are transmitted, then the values are processed, and the changed values are sent. This is followed by a gateway. After either informing the GO or sending the changed values, the process merges back to a single flow. After denying the request or after the merge, the readings are transmitted to the SP, and the process ends.
