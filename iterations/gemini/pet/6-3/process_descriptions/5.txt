The process starts when an order is received. Next, the necessary parts and delivery date are determined. This information is then entered into a Production Planning System (PPS). Based on this data, a procurement list is created and the data is transferred to an Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system. At this point, a decision is made: if parts are missing, the missing parts are procured; otherwise, if all parts are available, the parts availability is confirmed. After procuring missing parts or confirming availability, a system notice is received, followed by handling the implementation. Finally, the order quality is checked, and the process ends.
