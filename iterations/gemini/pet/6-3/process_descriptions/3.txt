The process starts when an order is received. Next, the necessary parts and delivery date are determined. This information is then entered into a Production Planning System (PPS). Based on this data, a procurement list is created and the data is transferred to an Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system. At this point, a decision is made: if parts are missing, the missing parts are procured. Otherwise, if all parts are available, the process continues directly. After procuring missing parts or confirming all parts are available, a notice is received from the system. Subsequently, the implementation is taken care of, and the order quality is checked. The process ends after the order quality is checked.
