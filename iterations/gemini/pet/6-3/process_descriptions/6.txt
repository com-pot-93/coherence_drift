The process starts when an order is received. Next, the necessary parts and delivery date are determined. This information is then entered into a Production Planning System (PPS). Based on this data, a procurement list is created, and the data is transferred to an Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system.
After the data transfer, a decision is made: if parts are missing, the missing parts are procured. Otherwise, if all parts are available, the parts availability is confirmed.
After procuring missing parts or confirming availability, a system notice is received, and the implementation is handled. Finally, the quality of the order is checked, and the process ends.
