The process starts when an order is received. Next, the necessary parts and delivery date are determined. This information is then entered into a Production Planning System (PPS). Based on this data, a procurement list is created and the data is transferred to an Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system.
After the data transfer, a decision is made based on parts availability. If parts are missing, the missing parts are procured. If all parts are available, the availability is confirmed.
After procuring missing parts or confirming availability, a system notice is received. Subsequently, the implementation is handled and the order quality is checked. The process ends after the order quality is checked.
