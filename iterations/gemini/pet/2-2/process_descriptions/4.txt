The process begins with receiving customer data. This data is then entered into a CRM system. The entered data is compared and checked for accuracy. If the data is not correct, it is corrected, otherwise, the process continues. A decision is then made whether to create a customer object. If the decision is yes, the customer object is created; otherwise, the process continues. Next, contract documents are prepared and then checked. If issues exist, they are analyzed and resolved; otherwise, the process continues. A confirmation and request are sent, and the request is checked. If the request is confirmed, suppliers are informed, and demand resolution is initiated; otherwise, the process continues. The agreement is then communicated and terminated. Following this, a decision is made whether to create a CIS contract. If the decision is yes, the CIS contract is created; otherwise, the process continues. The contract details are then checked, and meter data is received, completing the process.
