The process starts when customer data is received. This data is then entered into a CRM system. The entered data is compared against the original data to check for accuracy. If the data is not accurate, it is corrected, otherwise, the process continues. Next, a decision is made whether to create a customer object. If the decision is positive, the customer object is created; otherwise, the process continues. Then, contract documents are prepared and subsequently checked. If issues are found, they are analyzed and resolved; otherwise, the process continues. A confirmation and request are sent, and the request is checked. If the request is confirmed, suppliers are informed, and demand resolution is initiated; otherwise, the process continues. The agreement is then communicated and terminated. Following this, a decision is made on whether to create a CIS contract. If the decision is positive, the CIS contract is created; otherwise, the process continues. Finally, the contract details are checked, meter data is received, and the process ends.
