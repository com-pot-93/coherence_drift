The process starts with receiving customer data, followed by entering this data into a CRM system. The accuracy of the data is then compared. If the data is not accurate, it is corrected, otherwise, the process proceeds directly. Next, there is a decision point. If the answer is "yes", a customer object is created; otherwise, the process proceeds directly. Following this, contract documents are prepared and checked for any issues. If issues are found, they are analyzed and resolved; otherwise, the process proceeds directly. A confirmation and request are then sent, and the request is checked. If the request is confirmed, suppliers are informed, and demand resolution is initiated; otherwise, the process proceeds directly. Next, there is a decision point. If the answer is "yes", a CIS contract is created; otherwise, the process proceeds directly. Finally, the contract details are checked, meter data is received, and the process ends. If the request is not confirmed, the agreement is communicated and terminated.
