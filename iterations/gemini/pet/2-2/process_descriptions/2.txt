The process starts with receiving customer data. This data is then entered into a CRM system. The entered data is compared and checked for accuracy. If the data is not correct, it is corrected, otherwise, the process continues. Next, a decision is made whether to create a customer object. If the decision is positive, the customer object is created, otherwise, the process continues. Then, contract documents are prepared and subsequently checked. If there are issues with the contract documents, they are analyzed and resolved, otherwise, the process continues. A confirmation and request are sent, and the request is checked. If the request is confirmed, suppliers are informed, and demand resolution is initiated, otherwise, the process continues. The agreement is communicated and terminated, followed by a decision on whether to create a CIS contract. If the decision is positive, the CIS contract is created, otherwise, the process continues. Finally, the contract details are checked, meter data is received, and the process ends.
