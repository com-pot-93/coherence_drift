The process starts when a claim is received. An exclusive gateway then determines whether the insurance is active. If the insurance is active, the claim severity is evaluated, and relevant forms are sent. Another exclusive gateway then merges back into the process flow. A subsequent exclusive gateway then determines whether to proceed based on an unspecified condition. If the condition is true, the completeness of the forms is checked. Based on whether the forms are complete, as determined by another exclusive gateway, the process either informs the claimant to update the forms or registers the claim. Both paths then merge back before rejoining the previous gateway. If the insurance is inactive, the claimant is informed of the claim rejection. Finally, the process ends.
