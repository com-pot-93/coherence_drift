The process starts. Then, based on whether the insurance is active, the process either informs the claimant of claim rejection or evaluates the claim severity and sends forms. If the insurance is not active, the process merges back into the main flow. Otherwise, depending on whether condition A or condition B is met, the process either proceeds to the next step or checks the completeness of the forms. After that, the process merges back into the main flow. Next, based on whether the forms are complete, the process either registers the claim or informs the claimant to update the forms. Finally, the process ends.
