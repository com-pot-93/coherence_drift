The process starts when a claim is received. The claimant's insurance status is then checked. Depending on the outcome, the process either informs the claimant about the claim rejection or evaluates the claim severity and sends the claimant relevant forms. If the claimant returns the forms, they are checked for completeness. If the forms are incomplete, the claimant is informed to update them. Otherwise, the claim is registered in the Claims Management system. After either updating the forms or registering the claim, the process loops back to the point where the claimant returns the forms. If the claimant does not return the forms or the claimant is informed about claim rejection, the process ends.
