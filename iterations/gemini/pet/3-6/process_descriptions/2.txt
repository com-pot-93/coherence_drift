The process starts. A decision is made. If the insurance is inactive, the claimant is informed of the claim rejection. Otherwise, the claim severity is evaluated, and forms are sent. Another decision is made. If a condition is false, the process proceeds to the next step. Otherwise, the completeness of the forms is checked. A decision is made again. If the forms are complete, the claim is registered. Otherwise, the claimant is informed to update the forms. Finally, the process ends.
