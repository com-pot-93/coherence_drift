The process starts. A decision is made: if insurance is not active, the process proceeds to another decision point; otherwise, the claim severity is evaluated, and forms are sent. Following this, another decision is made: if condition A is met, the process proceeds to a subsequent decision point; if condition B is met, the completeness of the forms is checked before proceeding to the same decision point. This decision point then leads to another decision point. Here, if the forms are complete, the claim is registered before proceeding to another decision point; if the forms are incomplete, the claimant is informed to update the forms before proceeding to the same decision point. Finally, the process ends.
