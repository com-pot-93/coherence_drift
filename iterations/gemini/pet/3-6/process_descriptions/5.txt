The process starts. A decision is made. If the "Insurance Not Active" condition is met, the process proceeds to a merging gateway. Otherwise, the "Evaluate Claim Severity and Send Forms" task is performed, followed by another decision. If "Condition A" is met, the process proceeds to a merging gateway. If "Condition B" is met, the "Check Completeness of Forms" task is performed before proceeding to the same merging gateway. The process then continues to another merging gateway. Depending on whether "Forms Complete" or "Forms Incomplete," either the "Register Claim" task or the "Inform Claimant to Update Forms" task is performed, respectively. Both paths then converge at a merging gateway before the process ends.
