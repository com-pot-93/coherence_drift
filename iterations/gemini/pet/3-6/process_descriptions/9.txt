The process starts. A decision is made: if insurance is not active, the process proceeds to a merging gateway; otherwise, the claim severity is evaluated, and forms are sent. Following this, another decision is made: if condition A is met, the process proceeds to a merging gateway; if condition B is met, the form completeness is checked before proceeding to the merging gateway. The two paths merge into one, leading to another merging gateway. A decision is made: if the forms are complete, the claim is registered before proceeding to a merging gateway; if the forms are incomplete, the claimant is informed to update the forms before proceeding to the merging gateway. Finally, the two paths merge, and the process ends.
