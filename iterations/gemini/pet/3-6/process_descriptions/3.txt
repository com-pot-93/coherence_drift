The process starts. Then, based on whether the insurance is active, the process either informs the claimant of claim rejection or evaluates the claim severity and sends forms. In the first case, the process proceeds to a merging point. In the second case, depending on a condition, the process either proceeds to the next step or checks the completeness of the forms. Both options then proceed to the same merging point. From there, the process continues to another merging point. Next, depending on whether the forms are complete, the process either registers the claim or informs the claimant to update the forms. Finally, both options lead to the end of the process.
