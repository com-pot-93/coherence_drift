The process starts when MPON notifies MPOO about an equipment change. Then, a decision is made. If MPOO announces self-dismounting, it notifies MPON of this decision. Otherwise, MPOO notifies MPON that it will not self-dismount. After either of these notifications, MPON or MPOO performs a final reading, followed by the dismounting of the old equipment. MPON then mounts the new device and reads the meter count. MPON sends the final reading values to GO, which is then informed of the required data. Finally, GO notifies MSP about the required data, and the process ends.
