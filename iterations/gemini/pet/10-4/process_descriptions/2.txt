The process starts with notifying the MPOO of an equipment change. Subsequently, a decision is made. If the MPOO announces self-dismounting, the MPON is notified of the self-dismount. Otherwise, the MPON is notified that there will be no self-dismount. After either of these notifications, a final reading is performed, the old equipment is dismounted, and the new device is mounted. Then, the meter count is read, and the final reading is sent to the GO. Following this, the GO is informed of the required data, and the MSP is notified of the required data, completing the process.
