The process starts when an event occurs.
First, the 'Notify MPOO of equipment change' task is performed.
Then, a decision is made. If 'MPOO announces self-dismounting', the 'Notify MPON of self-dismount' task is performed.
Otherwise, if there is 'No self-dismounting', the 'Notify MPON of absence of self-dismount' task is performed.
After either of these tasks, the process continues to the 'Perform final reading' task.
Following this, the 'Dismount old equipment' task is performed, and then the 'Mount new device' task.
Next, the 'Read meter count' task is performed, followed by the 'Send final reading to GO' task.
Finally, the 'Inform GO and MSP of required data' task is performed, and the process ends.
