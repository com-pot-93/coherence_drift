The process starts when the MPON notifies the MPOO about intentions to change equipment. Subsequently, a decision is made. If the MPOO announces self-dismounting to the MPON, or alternatively, if the MPOO notifies the MPON that it will not self-dismount, the process continues. Following this, either the MPON or MPOO performs the final reading, and then dismounts the old equipment. The MPON then mounts the new device and reads the meter count from the installed meter. The MPON sends the final reading values to the GO and informs the GO of the required data. Finally, the GO notifies the MSP about the required data, and the process ends.
