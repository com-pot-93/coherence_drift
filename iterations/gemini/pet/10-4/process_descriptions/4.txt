The process starts with notifying the MPOO of an equipment change. Then, a decision is made. If the MPOO announces self-dismounting, the MPON is notified of the self-dismount. Otherwise, if there is no self-dismounting, the MPON is notified of the absence of self-dismount. After either of these notifications, a final reading is performed, the old equipment is dismounted, and the new device is mounted. Next, the meter count is read, and the final reading is sent to the GO. The GO and MSP are then informed of the required data, and the process ends.
