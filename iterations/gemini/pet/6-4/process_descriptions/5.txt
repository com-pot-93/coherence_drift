The process starts with determining contact details. Then, based on whether it is a purchase or a known person, the process either proceeds directly or identifies the interested person first. After identifying the interested person, or if it was a purchase, the customer is called, and the contact person and budget are determined. Next, the customer's interest is assessed. Following this, the process splits into two parallel paths.

In the first path, based on whether it is a sales rep presentation or a pre-sales presentation, the corresponding presentation is given. After either presentation, the process waits for the customer.

In the second parallel path, based on whether it is a sales rep presentation or a pre-sales presentation, the corresponding presentation is given. After either presentation, the process waits for the customer.

Once both paths are complete, based on whether it is a sales rep call or another sales rep call, the corresponding call is made. After either call, a quotation is created, and the process ends.
