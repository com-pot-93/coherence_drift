The process starts with the initiation of deregistration of a Mobile Point of Offer Origin (MPOO) at a government organization (GO). Subsequently, the deregistration is verified. Depending on the outcome of the verification, the process either rejects the deregistration or proceeds with preliminary confirmation. If rejected, the process ends. If preliminarily confirmed, the process prepares for readmission. Following preparation, the process diverges based on a condition. It can either proceed directly or confirm continuation to the MPOO. After either of these paths, the process converges and performs equipment acquisition or changes. Finally, the process ends.
