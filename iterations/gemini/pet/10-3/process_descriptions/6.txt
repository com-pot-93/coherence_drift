The process starts with the initiation of MPOO Deregistration. After this, the Deregistration is verified. Depending on the outcome of the verification, the process either proceeds along the 'Accepted' path or the 'Rejected' path. If accepted, a Preliminary Confirmation is performed, followed by preparation for Readmission, and then Equipment Acquisition or Changes are performed. Regardless of whether the 'Rejected' path was taken directly after verification or the 'Accepted' path was taken with its subsequent steps, the process ends.
