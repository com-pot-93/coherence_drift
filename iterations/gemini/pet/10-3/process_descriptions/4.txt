The process starts with the initiation of MPOO Deregistration. After this, the Deregistration is verified. Depending on the outcome of the verification, the process either proceeds if the verification is rejected, or it continues with performing a preliminary confirmation if the verification is accepted. In the first case, the process ends. In the second case, the process continues with preparing for readmission. Depending on the outcome of the preparation, the process either proceeds directly, or it continues with confirming continuation to MPOO. In the first case, the process continues with performing equipment acquisition or changes. In the second case, the process continues with performing equipment acquisition or changes. Finally, the process ends.
