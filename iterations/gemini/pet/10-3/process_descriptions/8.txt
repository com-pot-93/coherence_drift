The process starts with the initiation of MPOO Deregistration. After this, the Deregistration is verified. Depending on the outcome of the verification, the process follows one of two paths. If the Deregistration is accepted, a preliminary confirmation is sent, followed by preparation for readmission and equipment acquisition or changes. Alternatively, if the Deregistration is rejected, the process proceeds directly to the end. Finally, the process ends.
