The process starts with the initiation of MPOO Deregistration. After that, the Deregistration is verified. Depending on the outcome of the verification, the process follows one of two paths. If the Deregistration is accepted, a preliminary confirmation is sent, followed by preparation for readmission and equipment acquisition or changes. Alternatively, if the Deregistration is rejected, the process skips these steps. Regardless of whether the Deregistration was accepted or rejected, the process ends.
