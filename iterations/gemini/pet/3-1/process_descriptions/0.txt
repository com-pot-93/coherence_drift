The process starts when a party sends a warrant possession request. The Client Service Back Office receives the request and retrieves the SCT file. Then, the Client Service Back Office forwards the SCT Warrant Possession to Queensland Police, and the Back Office stores the SCT physical file. The Back Office then waits for a Police report and, upon receiving it, retrieves the respective SCT file. Following this, the Back Office attaches the new SCT document and stores the expanded SCT physical file, which concludes the process.
