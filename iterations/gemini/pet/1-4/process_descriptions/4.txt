The process starts. A decision is made. If the condition is met, underwriters are selected, a registration statement is prepared, and issue compliance is checked. Following this, two activities occur in parallel: the issue price is confirmed and a road show is arranged. Once both are completed, SEC clearance is received, the issue price is fixed, and a firm commitment is entered. Alternatively, if the condition is not met at the decision point, the process skips directly to offering stock to the public. Finally, the process ends.
