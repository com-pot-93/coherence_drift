The process starts. First, a decision is made to go public. Then, underwriters are selected, and a registration statement is prepared. After that, issue compliance is checked. Subsequently, two activities occur in parallel: the issue price is firmed up and a road show is arranged, and the approval of the registration statement is awaited. Once both activities are completed, clearance is received from the SEC. Following this, the issue price is fixed, and a firm commitment is entered. Finally, the stock is offered to the public, and the process ends.
