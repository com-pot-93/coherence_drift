The process starts when a claim is submitted. Then, the documents are checked, and the claim is registered. Next, the insurance is checked, followed by performing an assessment. Based on the assessment results, there is a decision to either authorize repairs or reject the claim. If repairs are authorized, the garage is called to authorize them, and then a payment is scheduled. If the claim is rejected, the claim is rejected. After either authorizing repairs and scheduling payment or rejecting the claim, a letter is sent to the customer, and the process ends.
