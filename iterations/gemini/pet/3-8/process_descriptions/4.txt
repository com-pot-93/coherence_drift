The process starts when a claim is submitted. The documents are then checked, and the claim is registered. Next, the insurance is checked, and an assessment is performed. Following the assessment, a decision is made: either authorize repairs or reject the claim. If repairs are authorized, the payment is scheduled. If the claim is rejected, a rejection task is performed. After either authorizing repairs and scheduling payment or rejecting the claim, a letter is sent to the customer, and the process ends.
