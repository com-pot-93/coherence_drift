The process starts when a claim is submitted. Then, the documents are checked, and the claim is registered. After that, the insurance is checked, and an assessment is performed. Depending on the outcome of the assessment, which can be either "Repairs Authorized" or "Claim Rejected," the process follows different paths. If "Repairs Authorized," a payment is scheduled. If "Claim Rejected," a rejection task is performed. After either scheduling the payment or performing the rejection task, a letter is sent, and the process ends.
