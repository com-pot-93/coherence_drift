The process starts when a customer submits a claim. The notification department then checks the documents and registers the claim. Next, the handling department picks up the claim and checks the insurance. After that, the handling department performs an assessment, which leads to a decision point. If the decision is to authorize repairs, the handling department phones the garage to authorize them and then schedules the payment. Alternatively, if the decision is to reject the claim, the handling department rejects it. Regardless of whether the claim is approved or rejected, the handling department sends a letter to the customer, and the process ends.
