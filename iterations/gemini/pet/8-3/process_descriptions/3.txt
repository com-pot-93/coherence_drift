The process starts with detecting personnel requirements. Subsequently, a vacancy is reported. Then, a decision is made. If the decision is affirmative, a job description is received, followed by another decision. From here, there are two possible paths: If corrections are needed, they are requested, and after the corrections are made, the process returns to the previous decision point. If no corrections are needed, the job description is approved, and the process returns to the previous decision point. Once the job description is finalized, the vacancy is advertised. After advertising the vacancy or if the initial decision was negative, the process ends.
