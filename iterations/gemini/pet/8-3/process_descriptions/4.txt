The process starts when personnel requirements are detected. Next, a vacancy is reported. Depending on whether a job description is available, the process either proceeds to receiving the job description or skips this step. If the job description is available, it is then reviewed. If corrections are needed, they are requested and subsequently made. This correction cycle repeats until no further corrections are necessary. Once the job description is approved or if no job description was available initially, the vacancy is advertised, and the process ends.
