The process starts with detecting personnel requirements. Then, a vacancy is reported to the personnel department. Subsequently, a decision is made. If the condition is true, a job description is received for review. Following this, another decision is made: if corrections are needed, they are requested; otherwise, the job description is approved. After either requesting corrections or approving the job description, the process continues to advertising the vacancy. The process loops back to the decision point where the condition is checked again. If the condition is not true, the process ends.
