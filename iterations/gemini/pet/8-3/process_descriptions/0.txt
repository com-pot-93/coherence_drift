The process starts when the HFD (presumably a department or role) detects a number of personnel requirements. The HFD then reports the vacancy to the Personnel Department. Subsequently, a decision is made. If the condition is true, the HFD receives the job description for review from the Personnel Department. Another decision point follows. From here, the process can either proceed with the HFD asking for corrections or with the HFD approving the job description, after which the HFD advertises the vacancy. After either of these paths, the process returns to the first decision point. If the initial condition is false, the process ends.
