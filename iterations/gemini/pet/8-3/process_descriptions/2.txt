The process starts when personnel requirements are detected. A vacancy is then reported to the personnel department. Subsequently, a decision is made. If the condition is true, a job description is received for review. Another decision is then made: if corrections are needed, they are requested; otherwise, the job description is approved. After either requesting corrections or approving the job description, the process advertises the vacancy and loops back to the previous decision point. If the initial condition is false, the process ends.
