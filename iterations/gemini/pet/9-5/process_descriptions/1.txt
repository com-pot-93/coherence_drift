The process starts, and then a decision is made based on Condition A.
If Condition A is not met, the company receives an expense report.
Then, a decision is made based on Condition E.
If Condition E is met, the company creates a new employee account.
Next, a decision is made based on Condition G.
If Condition G is not met, the company automatically approves the report.
Otherwise, the supervisor approves the report.
Then, a decision is made based on Condition I.
If Condition I is met, the company sends reimbursement to the employee's bank account.
Next, a decision is made based on Condition K.
If Condition K is met, the company stops the process, and the employee receives an email cancellation notice.
After that, the employee re-submits the expense report.
Otherwise, if Condition K is not met, a decision is made based on Condition L.
If Condition L is met, the employee receives an approval in progress email.
Otherwise, a decision is made based on Condition M.
If Condition M is not met, the employee receives a rejection notice.
Otherwise, the process ends.
