The process starts when a mortgage offer is sent. Following this, a decision is made based on whether a response is received. If no response is received, the process proceeds to another decision point. If a response is received, administration is performed, and a document request is generated, after which the process merges back to the aforementioned decision point. Regardless of the path taken up to this point, the case details are then updated, the work is archived, and a cancellation is performed, concluding the process.
