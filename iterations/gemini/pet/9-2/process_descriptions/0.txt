The process starts when a Customer Service Representative (CSR) sends a mortgage offer. The CSR then waits for a reply from the customer. Depending on the customer's response, the process follows one of two paths.

If the customer does not respond, the CSR updates the case details, archives the work, and performs a cancellation. After the cancellation, the process merges into a single flow.

Alternatively, if the customer responds, the CSR moves the case to administration. Following this, the CSR generates a request for any outstanding documents. The process then merges back, and the CSR updates the case details, archives the work, and performs a cancellation. After the cancellation, the process merges into a single flow.

Finally, the process ends.
