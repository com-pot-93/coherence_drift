The process starts when a mortgage offer is sent. Following this, a decision is made based on whether a response is received. If no response is received, the process proceeds to another decision point. If a response is received, administration is performed, followed by the generation of a document request, and then the process proceeds to the same decision point as in the 'no response' scenario. From this decision point, case details are updated, work is archived, a cancellation is performed, and the process ends.
