The process starts when a mortgage offer is sent. Subsequently, a decision is made based on whether a response is received. If no response is received, the case details are updated. Alternatively, if a response is received, an administration task is performed, followed by generating a document request and updating the case details. After either updating the case details directly (in the 'No Response' scenario) or after updating them following the document request (in the 'Response Received' scenario), the work is archived, and a cancellation is performed, which concludes the process.
