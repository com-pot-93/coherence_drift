The process starts. First, the database is backed up, and then it is checked. After the check, a decision is made. If the check was successful, the process proceeds to check the CRM system. If the check was not successful, a decision is made whether to send an alert to the supervisor. Depending on the outcome, the process proceeds to check the CRM system.

After checking the CRM system, a decision is made whether to register defaulting accounts and customers. Depending on the outcome, the process continues with a decision on whether to produce and send a table to account management. Depending on the outcome, the process continues with a decision on whether to perform reconciliation. Depending on the outcome, the process continues with a decision on whether to send a message to the supervisor. Depending on the outcome, the process ends.
