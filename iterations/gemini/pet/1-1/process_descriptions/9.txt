The process starts. A decision is made. If the decision is "Reject", the process ends. Otherwise, the process proceeds to a parallel execution.
In parallel, the process executes "Prepare for bike assembly" and "Process part list". After "Prepare for bike assembly" is completed, the process waits.
After "Process part list" is completed, a decision is made. If the decision is "Parts available", the process executes "Reserve parts". Otherwise, if the decision is "Parts not available", the process executes "Back-order parts".
After either "Reserve parts" or "Back-order parts" is completed, the process waits.
Once both "Prepare for bike assembly" and either "Reserve parts" or "Back-order parts" are completed, the process executes "Assemble and ship bicycle". Finally, the process ends.
