The process starts. A decision is made. If the decision is "Reject", the process ends. Otherwise, the process proceeds to a parallel execution.
In parallel, the process prepares for bike assembly and processes the part list. After the part list is processed, a decision is made based on parts availability. If parts are available, they are reserved. If parts are not available, they are back-ordered. Once the parts are either reserved or back-ordered, the process waits for the preparation for bike assembly to complete.
After both parallel paths are completed, the bicycle is assembled and shipped. Finally, the process merges back with the "Reject" branch and ends.
