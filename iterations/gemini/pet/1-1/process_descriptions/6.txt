The process starts. A decision is made. If the decision is to reject, the process ends. Otherwise, two activities occur in parallel: "Prepare for bike assembly" and "Process part list". After "Prepare for bike assembly" is completed, the process continues. After "Process part list" is completed, a decision is made. If parts are available, "Reserve parts" is performed. If parts are not available, "Back-order parts" is performed. After either "Reserve parts" or "Back-order parts" is completed, the process continues. After both parallel activities are completed, "Assemble and ship bicycle" is performed. Finally, the process ends.
