The process starts. A decision is made. If the order is rejected, the process ends. Otherwise, the process splits into two parallel activities: "Prepare for Bike Assembly" and "Process Part List." After "Prepare for Bike Assembly" is completed, the process waits for the completion of "Process Part List." In parallel, after "Process Part List" is completed, a decision is made based on parts availability. If parts are available, "Reserve Parts" is performed. If parts are not available, "Back-order Parts" is performed. After either "Reserve Parts" or "Back-order Parts" is completed, the process waits for the completion of "Prepare for Bike Assembly." Once both parallel activities are finished, "Assemble and Ship Bicycle" is performed. Finally, the process merges back and ends.
