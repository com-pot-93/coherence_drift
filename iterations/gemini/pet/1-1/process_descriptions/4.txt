The process starts. A decision is made. If the order is rejected, the process ends. Otherwise, the process proceeds to two parallel activities.
In parallel, the process prepares for bike assembly and processes the part list. After the preparation for bike assembly is complete, the process waits for the part list processing to finish.
When processing the part list, a decision is made based on parts availability. If the parts are available, they are reserved. If the parts are not available they are back-ordered. After either reserving or back-ordering the parts, the process waits for the preparation for bike assembly to complete.
Once both parallel activities are complete, the bicycle is assembled and shipped. Finally, the process ends.
