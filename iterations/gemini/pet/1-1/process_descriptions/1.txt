The process starts when an order is received. Based on the order, it is either rejected, or the storehouse and engineering departments are informed. If the storehouse and engineering departments are informed, two parallel activities are initiated: preparing for bike assembly and processing the part list. If the parts are available, they are reserved; otherwise, they are back-ordered. After the parts are either reserved or back-ordered, and the preparation for bike assembly is complete, the bicycle is assembled and then shipped. Finally, after the bicycle is shipped or the order is rejected, the process ends.
