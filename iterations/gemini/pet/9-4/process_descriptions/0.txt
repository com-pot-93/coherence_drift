The process begins with the start event.
First, the Coordination Unit finalizes the dates.
Next, the Support Officer updates all group calendars.
Then, the Support Officer creates meeting folders.
After that, the Support Officer ensures all appropriate documents are uploaded to the system.
Subsequently, the Support Officer advises Committee Members to read all documents.
Following this, the Committee Members hold a meeting.
After the meeting, the Support Office produces minutes.
Then, the Coordination Unit conducts a QA check on the minutes.
Next, the Coordination Unit sends the QA check to all Committee Members.
After that, the Support Officer updates departmental records.
The process concludes with the end event.
