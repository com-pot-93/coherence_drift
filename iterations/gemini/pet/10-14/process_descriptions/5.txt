The process starts. Depending on whether Condition A is met, the process either sends a bill to MPON or, if Condition B is met, sends the bill to GO.

If the bill was sent to MPON, it is then examined. If the bill is confirmed, an invoice is confirmed with payment advice, and the process merges into a single flow. If the bill is rejected, the invoice is rejected, and the process merges into a single flow. The process then merges into a single flow.

Alternatively, if the bill was sent to GO, it is examined. If the bill is rejected, the invoice is rejected, and the process merges into a single flow. If the bill is confirmed, an invoice is confirmed with payment advice, and the process merges into a single flow. The process then merges into a single flow.

The process merges into a single flow and ends.
