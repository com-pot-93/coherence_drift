The process starts. A decision is made: either the bill is sent to MPON or to GO.

If the bill is sent to MPON, it is then examined by MPON. Following the examination, another decision is made: either the bill is confirmed or rejected.

If the bill is confirmed, an invoice with payment advice is confirmed. After confirmation, the process merges into one path via a parallel gateway split and join.

If the bill is rejected, the invoice is rejected. After rejection, the process merges into one path via a parallel gateway split and join.

Both paths then merge into one via an exclusive gateway.

Alternatively, if the bill is sent to GO, it is examined by GO. Following the examination, a decision is made: either the bill is rejected or confirmed.

If the bill is rejected, the invoice is rejected. After rejection, the process merges into one path via a parallel gateway split and join.

If the bill is confirmed, an invoice with payment advice is confirmed. After confirmation, the process merges into one path via a parallel gateway split and join.

Both paths then merge into one via an exclusive gateway.

Finally, both main paths merge into one via an exclusive gateway, and the process ends.
