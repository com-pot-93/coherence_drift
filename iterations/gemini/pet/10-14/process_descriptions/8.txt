The process starts. Then, based on a condition, the process either sends a bill to MPON or sends a bill to GO.

If the bill is sent to MPON, MPON examines the bill. Depending on the examination result, the invoice is either confirmed with payment advice or rejected. Both outcomes then proceed to a merging point.

Alternatively, if the bill is sent to GO, GO examines the bill. Depending on the examination, the invoice is either rejected or confirmed with payment advice. Both outcomes then proceed to a merging point.

Finally, the process ends after the two alternative paths have merged.
