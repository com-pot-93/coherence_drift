The process starts when the process is initiated. First, the files are checked. Based on the outcome, the process either proceeds when the files are okay, or when files are missing. If the files are okay, they are physically tracked. If the files are missing, a search is initiated to find the missing files. After either physically tracking the files or searching for missing files, the process continues. Next, two activities occur in parallel: handing the files to an associate and distributing the judge's law list. After both activities are completed, a directions hearing is conducted, and the process ends.
