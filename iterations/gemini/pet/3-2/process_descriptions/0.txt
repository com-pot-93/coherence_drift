The process starts. First, the files are checked. Based on the outcome of the check, either the files are physically tracked, or a search for missing files is initiated. After either of these activities is completed, the process continues to two parallel activities. In the first one, the files are handed to the Associate. In parallel, the Judge's Lawlist is distributed to the relevant people. Once both activities are completed, the directions hearings are conducted, and the process ends.
