The process starts when the process is initiated. First, the files are checked. If the files are okay, they are physically tracked. If the files are missing, a search is conducted to find the missing files. After the files are physically tracked or after searching for the missing files, the process proceeds to two parallel activities. In the first activity, the files are handed to an associate. In the second activity, the judge's lawlist is distributed. After both activities are completed, a directions hearing is conducted, and the process ends.
