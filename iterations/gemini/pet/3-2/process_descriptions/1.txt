The process starts when the process is initiated. First, the files are checked. Depending on whether the files are okay or missing, the process follows one of two alternative paths. If the files are okay, they are physically tracked. If the files are missing, a search is conducted to find them. After physically tracking the files or searching for the missing files, the process continues to a point where two activities occur in parallel: handing the files to an Associate and distributing the Judge's Lawlist. Once both activities are completed, a directions hearing is conducted, and the process ends.
