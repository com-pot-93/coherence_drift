The process starts with the registration of a claim. The claim is then examined. Based on the examination, a decision is made. If the condition is true, a settlement recommendation is written, which is then checked. After the recommendation is checked, the claim is marked. Another decision is made at this point. If the decision is to send the claim back, it is sent back, and the process loops back to the decision point after the examination. Alternatively, if the decision is to proceed, the claim is handled, and the process loops back to the same decision point. If the initial condition after the examination is not true, the process ends.
