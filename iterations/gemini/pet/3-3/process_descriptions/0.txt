The process starts when the system registers a claim. Next, a claims officer examines the claim. Based on the examination, a decision is made. If the decision is true, the claims officer writes a settlement recommendation, which is then checked by a senior claims officer. The senior claims officer then marks the claim. Following this, another decision is made. Depending on the outcome, the system either sends the claim back or proceeds with the claim handling process. After either of these actions, the process loops back to the first decision point. If the initial decision is not true, the process ends.
