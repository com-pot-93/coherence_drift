The process starts when a vacancy is reported to Human Resources (HR). HR then creates a job description. Following this, there is a decision point. If necessary, HR asks the relevant department for clarification; otherwise, this step is skipped. The process then merges back together. Another decision point follows. HR submits the job description for consideration and waits for approval.

Subsequently, there is another decision point. If the department requests a correction, HR corrects the description, and the process merges again. Otherwise, the correction step is skipped. The process merges back.

If the job description is approved, HR posts the job, and the process ends.
