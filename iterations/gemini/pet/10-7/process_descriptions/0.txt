The process starts when the MSPN registers a measurement at the GO.
Next, the GO examines the application of the MSPN.
Based on the examination results, the process follows one of two alternative paths.
In the first path, the GO rejects the application of the MSPN.
In the second path, the GO confirms the application of the MSPN.
Both paths converge, and the GO assigns the MSPN.
After the assignment, the GO informs the MSPO, the MPO, and the SP about the MSPN assignment, in that order.
The process ends after the SP is informed.
