The process starts when an MSP Operator deregisters at the Government Organization.
The Government Organization then verifies the deregistration.
Based on the verification, the process follows one of two paths.
In the first path, the Government Organization rejects the deregistration.
In the second path, the Government Organization preliminarily confirms the deregistration.
Both paths then converge, and the Government Organization assigns itself as the MSP.
Following this, the Government Organization informs the MSP Operator about the state of the assignment, then informs the MPO, and finally informs the SP about the assignment.
The process then ends.
