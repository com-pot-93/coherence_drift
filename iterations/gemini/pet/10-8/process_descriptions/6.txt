The process starts with the initiation of a deregistration. Subsequently, the deregistration is verified. Depending on the outcome of the verification, the process either proceeds along the 'Confirmed' path or the 'Rejected' path. If the deregistration is confirmed, a government organization is assigned as the MSP (Managed Service Provider), the MSP operator is informed, the MPO (Mobile Program Operator) is informed, and finally, the SP (Service Provider) is informed. Regardless of whether the deregistration was rejected or went through the confirmation steps, the process ends.
