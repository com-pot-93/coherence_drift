The process starts with the initiation of a deregistration task. Following this, the deregistration is verified. Depending on the outcome of the verification, the process diverges. If the deregistration is confirmed, a government organization is assigned as the MSP, after which the MSP operator, the MPO, and the SP are informed in sequence. Alternatively, if the deregistration is rejected, a notification of the deregistration rejection is sent. Regardless of whether the deregistration was confirmed or rejected, the process concludes with an end event.
