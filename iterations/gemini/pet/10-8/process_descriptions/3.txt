The process starts with the initiation of a deregistration procedure. Subsequently, the deregistration is verified. Based on the verification outcome, the process either rejects the deregistration or preliminarily confirms it. In case of rejection, the process proceeds directly to the end. If the deregistration is preliminarily confirmed, a government organization is assigned as the MSP (Managed Service Provider). Following this assignment, the MSP operator, the MPO (Mobile Phone Operator), and the SP (Service Provider) are informed in sequence. The process then ends.
