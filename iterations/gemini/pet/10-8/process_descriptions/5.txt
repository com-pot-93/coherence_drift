The process starts with the initiation of a deregistration task. Following this, the deregistration is verified. Depending on the outcome of the verification, the process diverges. If the deregistration is rejected, the process ends. Alternatively, if the deregistration is confirmed, a government organization is assigned as the MSP (Managed Service Provider). Subsequently, the MSP operator, the MPO (Mobile Payment Operator), and the SP (Service Provider) are informed in sequence. Finally, after all notifications, the process ends.
