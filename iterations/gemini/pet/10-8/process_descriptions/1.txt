The process starts with a start event. Then, a task to verify deregistration is performed. Based on the outcome of the verification, the process either rejects the deregistration or preliminarily confirms it. If the deregistration is rejected, a task to reject the deregistration is performed. If the deregistration is preliminarily confirmed, a task to preliminarily confirm the deregistration is performed. After either rejecting or preliminarily confirming the deregistration, a task to assign a government organization as the MSP is performed. Following this, a task to inform the MSP operator is performed, after which a task to inform the MPO is performed. Next, a task to inform the SP is performed, and the process ends with an end event.
