The process starts with the initiation of a deregistration task. Following this, the deregistration is verified. Depending on the outcome of the verification, the process proceeds along one of two exclusive paths. If the deregistration is confirmed, the government organization is assigned as the MSP, and the MSP operator, MPO, and SP are informed in sequence. Alternatively, if the deregistration is rejected, a deregistration rejection notification is sent. Regardless of whether the deregistration was confirmed or rejected, the process concludes with an end event.
