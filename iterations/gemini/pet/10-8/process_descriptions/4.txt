The process starts with the initiation of a deregistration task. Following this, a verification of the deregistration is performed. The process then branches based on a decision point. If the deregistration is rejected, the process proceeds to an end point. Alternatively, if the deregistration is confirmed, a government organization is assigned as the MSP (Managed Service Provider). Subsequently, the MSP operator, the MPO (Mobile Payment Operator), and the SP (Service Provider) are informed in sequence. Finally, the process merges back to a single flow before reaching the end point.
