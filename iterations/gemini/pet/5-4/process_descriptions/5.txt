The process starts when a user initiates the process.
First, the user purchases a product, then submits an expense report.
After submitting the expense report, a decision is made. If the report requires review, it is sent for supervisor review, otherwise, the process skips directly to the report approval.
After the supervisor review or if no review is required, the report is approved.
Following the approval, two activities occur simultaneously: the process proceeds to an escalation task and a check receipts task.
For the check receipts task, a decision is made. Depending on the outcome, the report is either edited, escalated, or the payment is confirmed. Only one of these three options is chosen.
After editing, escalating, or confirming the payment, the process waits for the escalation task to complete.
Once both the escalation task and the selected path from the check receipts task are completed, the process escalates and then ends.
