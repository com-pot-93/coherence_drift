The process starts when an employee purchases a product. Next, the employee submits an expense report. Depending on the outcome of an evaluation, the process either proceeds directly to a merge point, or the supervisor reviews the report and then approves it, after which the process continues to the same merge point. From there, depending on the outcome of an evaluation, the process either proceeds directly to another merge point, or the treasurer checks the receipts, leading to another decision point. If the expenses are accepted, the payment is confirmed. Alternatively, the report can be sent back for editing, or the process can be escalated. In any of these three cases, the process returns to the previous merge point. From there, the process loops back to the merge point mentioned earlier. Finally, the process ends.
