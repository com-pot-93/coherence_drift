The process starts with the initiation of purchasing a product. Subsequently, an expense report is submitted. A decision is then made: if the report requires review, it is sent for supervisor review, otherwise, the process skips directly to the report approval. After the supervisor review, the process continues to the report approval step. Following the approval, the process splits into two parallel paths: one path involves an escalation task, and the other path involves checking receipts. After the escalation task is completed, the process waits for the completion of the other path. In the receipts path, a decision is made to either edit the report, escalate the report, or confirm payment. After any of these three options is completed, the process waits for the completion of the other path. After checking receipts, the process merges back to a single path, and an escalation is performed. The process concludes with the end event.
