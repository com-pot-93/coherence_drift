The process starts with the initiation of purchasing. Following this, an expense report is submitted. A decision is then made: if the report requires review, it undergoes a supervisor review; otherwise, this step is skipped. Subsequently, the report is approved. After approval, two parallel paths are initiated: one involves an escalation task, and the other involves checking receipts. During the receipt checking, a decision is made: the process can either proceed with editing the report, escalating the report, or confirming payment. After any of these three options, the process merges back to a single flow. Once both the escalation task and the receipt checking (including its subsequent decision) are completed, another escalation task is performed, and the process ends.
