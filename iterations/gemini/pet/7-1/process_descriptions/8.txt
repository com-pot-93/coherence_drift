The process starts. First, the open leads are checked. Then, the top five leads are selected. Next, these leads are contacted. An exclusive gateway then determines the subsequent flow. If a condition is met, the process continues to another exclusive gateway; otherwise, it proceeds directly to the end event. If the condition is met, the customer is called. Another exclusive gateway then determines the next step. If the answer is "Yes", a note is sent to the manager, and the lead is processed. After processing the lead, the process merges back. If the answer is "No", the process merges back immediately. The process returns to the first exclusive gateway. If the condition is not met, the process ends.
