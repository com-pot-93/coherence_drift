The process starts. First, the open leads are checked. Then, the top five leads are selected. Next, these leads are contacted. Based on the outcome, a decision is made. If the condition is true, the customer is called. Following the call, another decision is made. If the answer is yes, a note is sent to the manager, and the lead is processed. After processing the lead, the process loops back to the earlier decision point. If the answer is no, the process also loops back to the same decision point. If the initial condition was not true, the process ends.
