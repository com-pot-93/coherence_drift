The process starts. First, the open leads are checked. Then, the top five leads are selected. Next, these leads are contacted. Following this, a decision is made. If the condition is true, the customer is called. After calling the customer, another decision is made based on the answer. If the answer is yes, a note is sent to the manager, and the lead is processed. Regardless of whether the answer is yes or no, the process returns to the first decision point. If the condition is false, the process ends.
