The process starts when the Manager checks the open leads. Next, the Manager selects the top five leads and tells the Sales Assistant to contact the leads.
After that, a decision is made. If the condition is true, the Sales Assistant calls the customer. Then, another decision is made. If the condition is met, the Sales Assistant sends a note to the Manager, who then processes the lead. After the lead is processed or if the condition is not met, the process returns to the previous decision point.
If the condition at the first decision point is false, the process ends.
