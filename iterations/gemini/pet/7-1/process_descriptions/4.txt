The process starts. First, the open leads are checked. Then, the top five leads are selected. Next, these leads are contacted. Following this, a decision is made. If the condition is true, the customer is called. After calling the customer, another decision is made. If the answer is yes, a note is sent to the manager, and the lead is processed. After processing the lead, the process returns to the first decision point. If the answer to the second decision is no, the process also returns to the first decision point. If the condition at the first decision point is false, the process ends.
