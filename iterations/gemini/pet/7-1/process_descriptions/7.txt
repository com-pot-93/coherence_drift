The process starts. First, the open leads are checked. Then, the top five leads are selected. Next, these leads are contacted. After contacting the leads, a decision is made. If the condition is true, the customer is called. After calling the customer, another decision is made based on the answer. If the answer is yes, a note is sent to the manager, and the lead is processed. After processing the lead, the process returns to the first decision point. If the answer is no, the process also returns to the first decision point. If the initial condition is not true, the process ends.
