The process starts. First, the mail is collected. Then, the collected mail is sorted and distributed. Next, the mail is opened, sorted, and registered. After that, a quality check is performed. If the quality check fails, requisitions are compiled and sent. Otherwise, if the quality check is successful, matter details are captured, and fees are taken. Subsequently, two activities occur in parallel: receipt is put and documents are posted, and party details are captured and a file is printed. After both parallel activities are completed, the process merges back to a single flow. Finally, the process ends.
