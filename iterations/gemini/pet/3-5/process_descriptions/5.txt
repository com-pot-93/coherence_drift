The process starts with collecting mail, followed by sorting and distributing it. Then, the mail is opened, sorted, and registered. A quality check is performed, and based on the outcome, the process either compiles and sends requisitions or captures matter details and takes fees. If the quality check passes and requisitions are compiled and sent, the process proceeds to the end. If matter details are captured and fees are taken, the process splits into two parallel activities: putting the receipt and posting documents, and capturing party details and printing the file. After both parallel activities are completed, the process merges back and proceeds to the end.
