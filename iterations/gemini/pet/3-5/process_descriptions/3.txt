The process starts. First, the mail is collected. Then, the collected mail is sorted and distributed. Next, the mail is opened, sorted, and registered. After that, a quality check is performed. Depending on the outcome of the quality check, the process either compiles and sends requisitions or captures matter details and takes fees. If the outcome is positive, requisitions are compiled and sent. Otherwise, if the outcome is negative, the matter details are captured, and fees are taken. In the case where matter details are captured and fees are taken, two activities occur in parallel: putting the receipt and posting documents, and capturing party details and printing the file. After both of these activities are completed, the process continues. Finally, the process ends.
