The process starts. First, the mail is collected. Then, the collected mail is sorted and distributed. Next, the mail is opened, sorted, and registered. After that, a quality check is performed. Depending on the outcome of the quality check, either requisitions are compiled and sent, or matter details are captured and fees are taken. If requisitions are compiled and sent, the process continues to the next step. If matter details are captured and fees are taken, two activities occur in parallel: a receipt is put and documents are posted, and party details are captured and a file is printed. Once both of these activities are completed, the process continues to the next step. Finally, the process ends.
