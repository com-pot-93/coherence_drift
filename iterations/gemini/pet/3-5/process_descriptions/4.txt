The process starts with collecting mail. Then, the collected mail is sorted and distributed. Next, the mail is opened, sorted, and registered. After that, a quality check is performed. Depending on the outcome of the quality check, the process either proceeds with compiling and sending requisitions or capturing matter details and taking fees.

If the process proceeds with capturing matter details and taking fees, then two activities are performed in parallel: putting the receipt and posting documents, and capturing party details and printing the file. After both activities are completed, the process continues.

Finally, the process ends after compiling and sending requisitions or after the parallel execution is completed.
