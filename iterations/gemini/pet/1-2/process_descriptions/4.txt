The process starts when a defect is reported. Then, the repair cost is calculated. Based on the outcome, there are two possible paths. Either the repair is declined, and the computer is taken home, or the repair is approved, and the process continues with parallel activities. If the repair is approved, the hardware is repaired, and the software is configured simultaneously. After both tasks are completed, the system functionality is tested. Finally, after testing or if the repair was declined, the process ends.
