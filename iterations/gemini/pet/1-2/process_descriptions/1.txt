The process starts when a defect is reported and the repair cost is calculated. Subsequently, a decision is made based on whether the customer declines the repair. If the customer declines, they take the computer home, and the process ends. Otherwise, hardware and software checks are performed in parallel. The hardware check involves repairing the hardware, while the software check involves configuring the software. After both checks are completed, the system functionality is tested. Finally, the process ends.
