The process starts with determining the required resources. Then, two activities occur in parallel: checking the repository for already available resources and collecting urgently needed resources. After both are completed, a decision is made. If the resources are not sufficient, tools are built; otherwise, this step is skipped. Next, the remaining resources are collected, and two activities occur in parallel: evaluating a condition and updating the list of resources.
If the condition is true, the process waits for a random natural event for some time. After that, a decision is made between resetting the bot and continuing farming, or starting farming.
After farming, a decision is made. If the condition is true, the player is notified and reads the message; otherwise, this step is skipped.
After that, a decision is made. If the condition is true, the bot is given a sleeping break. Otherwise, a decision is made between bragging to friends and helping with materials.
After any of these options, the process returns to the condition evaluation step.
If the condition is false, the process ends.
