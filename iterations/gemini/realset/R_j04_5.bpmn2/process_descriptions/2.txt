The process starts. A task is performed, and then a decision is made. If the decision is "Yes," a car owner is notified, and another decision is made. If this second decision is "Yes," a task is performed, and the car owner is fined. If the second decision is "No," car problems are received, and a payment is offered. A third decision is then made. If this third decision is "Yes," a payment is transferred. If the third decision is "No," the process proceeds to a merging point. After the payment transfer or the negative decision, the process merges to a second merging point. After the car owner is fined or after the second merging point, the process merges to a first merging point. If the first decision is "No," the process proceeds directly to the first merging point. After the first merging point, a repair confirmation is received, followed by receiving the next service time. The process then ends.
