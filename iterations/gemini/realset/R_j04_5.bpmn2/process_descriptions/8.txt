The process starts. A task is performed, after which a decision is made. If the decision is negative, the process proceeds to another decision point. If the decision is positive, a notification is sent, followed by another decision. If this decision is positive, another task is performed, and then the process proceeds to the earlier decision point. If the decision is negative, the process also proceeds to the earlier decision point. From there, a task is performed to receive car problems, followed by offering a payment. Another decision is made. If the decision is positive, a payment is transferred, and the process moves to another point. If the decision is negative, the process directly moves to that point. Subsequently, a repair confirmation is received, followed by receiving the next service time, and then the process ends.
