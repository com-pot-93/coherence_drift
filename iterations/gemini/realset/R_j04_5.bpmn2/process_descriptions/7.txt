The process starts. A task is performed, after which a decision is made. If the decision is negative, the process continues; otherwise, if the decision is positive, a notification is sent. After the notification, another decision is made. If this decision is positive, a task is performed, and then the process continues; otherwise, if the decision is negative, the process continues. The process continues from the point where either the negative decision was made or after the task was performed. The process also continues from the point where the first decision was negative. The process then proceeds with receiving car problems, followed by offering a payment. Subsequently, another decision is made. If the decision is positive, a payment is transferred, and then the process continues; otherwise, if the decision is negative, the process continues. The process continues from the point where either the negative decision was made or after the payment was transferred. The process continues with receiving repair confirmation and then receiving the next service time, after which the process ends.
