The process starts. A task is performed, and then a decision is made. If the decision is positive, a car owner is notified, and another decision is made. If this second decision is positive, a task is performed before proceeding. If either the second decision is negative or the task is completed, the process continues. If the initial decision is negative, the process skips the notification and task, and proceeds directly. Another task is performed to receive car problems, followed by offering a payment. A decision is then made regarding the payment offer. If the decision is positive, a payment is transferred before proceeding. If the decision is negative, the payment transfer is skipped. The process then receives repair confirmation and the next service time, before it ends.
