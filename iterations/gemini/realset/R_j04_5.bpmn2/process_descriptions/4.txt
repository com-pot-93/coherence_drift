The process starts. A task is performed, after which a decision is made. If the decision is "Yes", a car owner is notified, and another decision is made. If this second decision is "Yes", a task is performed, and the car owner is fined. If the second decision is "No", or after the car owner is fined, the process continues. If the first decision is "No", the process skips notifying the car owner and fining them.
Following this, car problems are received, and a payment is offered. Another decision is made. If the decision is "Yes", a payment is transferred. If the decision is "No", or after the payment is transferred, a repair confirmation is received, followed by receiving the next service time. The process then ends.
