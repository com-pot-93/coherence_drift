The process starts. A task is performed, and then a decision is made. If the decision is "Yes," a car owner is notified, and another decision is made. If this second decision is "Yes," a task is performed, and the car owner is fined. If either the second decision is "No," or after the car owner is fined, the process continues. If the first decision is "No," the process skips notifying the car owner and fining them. The process continues with receiving car problems, offering payment, and making another decision. If the decision is "Yes," a payment is transferred. If the decision is "No," or after the payment is transferred, the process continues. The process continues with receiving repair confirmation, receiving the next service time, and then the process ends.
