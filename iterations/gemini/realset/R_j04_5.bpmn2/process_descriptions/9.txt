The process starts. A task is performed, after which a decision is made. If the decision is negative, the process continues to another decision point. Otherwise, a notification is sent, and another decision is made. If this decision is positive, another task is performed before proceeding to the other decision point. If the decision is negative, the process proceeds directly to the other decision point. From there, a task is performed to receive car problems, followed by a task to offer payment. Another decision is made. If the decision is positive, a payment is transferred before proceeding to another step. If the decision is negative, the process proceeds directly to that step. A repair confirmation is received, followed by receiving the next service time, and then the process ends.
