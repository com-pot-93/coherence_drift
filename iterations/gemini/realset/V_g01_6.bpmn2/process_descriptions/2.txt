The process starts. Then, a decision is made. If the "Only burger" option is selected, the process proceeds to another decision point. Otherwise, the process splits into two parallel paths.

In the first parallel path, a drink is prepared.

In the second parallel path, a decision is made. Depending on the choice, either wedges or fries are prepared. After preparing either wedges or fries, the process merges back to a single flow.

The two parallel paths then merge. The process proceeds to another decision point. After the decision, the process splits into two parallel paths again.

In the first parallel path, a burger is prepared.

In the second parallel path, a loop is initiated. The process enters a decision point, which leads to another decision point. From there, if "Enthusiastic updates" is selected, enthusiastic status updates are given, followed by escalation. The process then loops back to the initial decision point of this parallel path. Otherwise, the process proceeds to the merge point.

The two parallel paths merge. Finally, the order is put on a conveyor belt, and the process ends.
