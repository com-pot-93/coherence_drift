The process starts and immediately encounters a decision point.

If the condition is true, the process proceeds to another decision point. If this second condition is true, a status is sent, and the process moves to a third decision point. If this third condition is true, an object is printed before the process merges back. If the third condition is false, the process merges back directly.

If the second condition is false, the process merges back directly.

If the initial condition is false, the process ends.
