The process starts and proceeds to a decision point. If the condition is met, the process continues to another decision point. If this second condition is met, a status is sent, and the process advances to another decision point. If this third condition is met, an object is printed before the process proceeds to another decision point. Otherwise, the process skips the printing task and proceeds directly to that decision point. This decision point then leads to another decision point. If the second condition is not met, the process skips sending the status and printing the object, proceeding directly to the same decision point. This decision point then leads to another decision point. If the initial condition is not met, the process bypasses all previous tasks and decision points, proceeding directly to the final decision point. Finally, the process ends.
