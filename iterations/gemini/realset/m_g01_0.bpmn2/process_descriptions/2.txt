The process starts, and then proceeds to a decision point.
Depending on the outcome of the decision, the process either proceeds to another decision point or it ends.
If the process proceeds to the second decision point, depending on the outcome, the process either continues to a 'Send Status' task or it merges into another gateway.
After the 'Send Status' task is completed, the process continues to another decision point.
Depending on the outcome of the decision, the process either executes a 'Print Object' task or merges into another gateway.
After the 'Print Object' task is completed, the process merges into another gateway.
The two possible paths from the previous decision point converge into a gateway, which then merges into another gateway.
The alternative path from the second decision point merges into the same gateway.
From there, the process returns to the first decision point, forming a loop.
If the process takes the other path from the first decision point, the process ends.
