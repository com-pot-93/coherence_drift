The process starts. First, the machine is run, and then the connection is checked. If the connection check is positive, the process continues; otherwise, measurements are collected before proceeding. Next, a check is performed to see if a certain condition is met. If it is, the process continues; if not, the OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness) is calculated. Following this, the process splits into two parallel activities: the machine is stopped, and an email is sent to an engineer. Once both activities are completed, the process ends.
