The process starts with the creation of a list of necessary parts, followed by querying webshops for these parts. The cheapest shop is then chosen, and an order is placed.

After placing the order, the webshop receives it. Simultaneously, the webshop sends an invoice, delivers the order, and updates its inventory.

Once these parallel activities are completed, the manufacturer receives the order and starts building.
