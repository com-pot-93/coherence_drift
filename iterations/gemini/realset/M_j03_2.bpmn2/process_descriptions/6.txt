The process starts. First, a parts list is created, and then webshops are queried. After that, the cheapest shop is chosen, and an order is placed. Next, the process splits into four parallel activities: receiving the order, sending the invoice, delivering the order, and updating the inventory. Once all four activities are completed, the order is received again, and the building process starts. Then, a decision is made based on whether a condition is met. If the condition is met, the process proceeds directly to the next decision. If not, a faster delivery is ordered before proceeding. Subsequently, there are two consecutive exclusive gateways. In the first one, if a condition D is met, a compliment email is written; otherwise, the cheapest part is ordered. In the second one, if a condition E is met, the cheapest part is ordered; otherwise, the process is escalated. Finally, the process ends.
