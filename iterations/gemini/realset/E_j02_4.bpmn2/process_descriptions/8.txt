The process starts. Then, two activities occur in parallel: "Check Baby Age" and "Check Parent Status". After both activities are completed, a decision is made. If the parent is "Employed," a "Send Notification to Employer" task is performed. Otherwise, if the parent is "Self-Employed/Studying/Housewife/Man," this task is skipped. Finally, "Parents Take Leave" task is performed, and the process ends.
