The process starts. Then, two tasks, "Check Baby Age" and "Check Parent Status," are performed in parallel. After both tasks are completed, a decision is made based on employment status.

If the person is "Employed," a notification is sent to the employer, followed by receiving a confirmation and then taking maternity leave. Otherwise, if the person is "Self-Employed/Studying/Housewife/Man," this step is skipped.

Next, a decision is made, and depending on the path taken, a notification is sent to the employer.

Following this, another decision is made, leading to either performing "Task A" or "Task B." After either task is completed, an employer notification is received, and maternity leave is taken.

Alternatively, after the first decision, a different notification is sent to the employer.

Subsequently, another decision is made, leading to either performing "Task C" or "Task D." After either task is completed, an employer notification is received, and maternity leave is taken.

As another alternative after the first decision, "Parents Take Leave."

Finally, after any of the paths converge, the process continues, and then it ends.
