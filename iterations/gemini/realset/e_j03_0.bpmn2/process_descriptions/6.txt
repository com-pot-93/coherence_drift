The process starts with the initiation of an assessment tool. Subsequently, the tool is started, and the incident type is entered. The incident is then specified. Depending on the situation, either accident insurance and the principal are contacted, private insurance is contacted, or user/victim data is entered. Following this, a decision is made, leading to a parallel execution of contacting accident insurance and, based on a condition, contacting the labor inspectorate. After both parallel paths are completed, another decision is made. If the answer is yes, or after the parallel execution, superiors are contacted. Finally, tasks are proposed to the user, the proposed task is executed, and the process ends.
