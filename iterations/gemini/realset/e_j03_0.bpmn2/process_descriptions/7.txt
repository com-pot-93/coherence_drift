The process starts with initiating an assessment tool, followed by starting the tool. The next step is to enter the incident type and then specify the incident. Based on the incident specification, one of three paths is chosen: either contacting accident insurance and the principal, contacting private insurance, or entering user/victim data. After any of these actions, a decision is made. If the decision requires further action, two parallel paths are initiated: contacting accident insurance and contacting the labor inspectorate. Once both are completed, or if the decision does not require further action, superiors are contacted. Finally, tasks are proposed to the user, the proposed task is executed, and the process ends.
