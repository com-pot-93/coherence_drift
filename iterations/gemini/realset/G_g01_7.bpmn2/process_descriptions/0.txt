The process starts with sending invitations to friends. After the invitations are sent, a question is sent to the friends asking which games they want to play. The list of games is then received, and the games are chosen.

Following this, the process splits into parallel paths.

In the first path, a date for the LAN party is proposed, and a request to send it is made. If the date needs to be changed, a date change task is performed, and a new date for the LAN party is proposed.

In the second parallel path, guests are asked which games they want to play. If the guest games need to be changed, a task to change guest games is performed, and the guests are asked again which games they want to play.

After both of the above paths are completed, the process continues with downloading the games.

Next, a question is sent to the friends asking how much beer they want to drink. After receiving the estimated number of beers required, a decision is made whether to go to the supermarket. If it is decided to go to the supermarket, the missing beer is bought.

Finally, preparations for the LAN party are made, and the process ends.
