The process starts. Then, a decision is made. If the answer is "Yes", the process continues with entering account information. Otherwise, if the answer is "No", the process continues with logging into the system. After either entering account information or logging into the system, the process continues. Next, a confirmation email is received, and then account information is loaded. Another decision is made. If the answer is "Yes", a payment method is selected. Following this, another decision is made based on the payment method. If the payment method is "Credit Card", credit card information is entered. If the payment method is "IBAN/BIC", IBAN/BIC information is entered. After entering either credit card or IBAN/BIC information, the process continues. If the answer to the previous decision is "No", the process skips the payment steps. After the payment steps or skipping them, the process continues with logging into the game. Subsequently, the process splits into three parallel paths: selecting a realm, selecting a race, and selecting a class. After all three paths are completed, the process merges back into a single flow. The character is then named, followed by receiving confirmation and selfies, and the process ends.
