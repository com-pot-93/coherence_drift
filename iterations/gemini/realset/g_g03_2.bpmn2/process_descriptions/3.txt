The process starts. A decision is made. If the answer is "Yes", account information is entered, a confirmation email is received, and a link in the email is clicked. Otherwise, if the answer is "No", the process skips directly to logging into Battle.net. After clicking the link or skipping the previous steps, the user logs into Battle.net and loads account information. Another decision is made. If the answer is "Yes", a payment method is selected. Depending on the payment method, either credit card information or IBAN/BIC information is entered. After entering either the credit card or IBAN/BIC information, or if the answer to the previous decision is "No", the user logs into the game. In parallel, the user selects a realm, selects a race, and selects a class. After all three selections are made, the character is named, and a confirmation along with selfies is received. The process ends.
