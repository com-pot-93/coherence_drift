The process starts. A decision is made. If the answer is "Yes", account information is entered, a confirmation email is received, and a link in the email is clicked, leading to a merge point. If the answer is "No", the process skips directly to the same merge point. From the merge point, the process continues by logging into Battle.net and loading account information. Another decision is made. If the answer is "Yes", a payment method is selected. Depending on whether a credit card or an IBAN/BIC is chosen, the corresponding information is entered, leading to a merge point. From the merge point, the process continues to another merge point. If the answer to the previous decision is "No", the process skips directly to the same merge point. From the merge point, the process continues by logging into the game. Then, in parallel, the realm, race, and class are selected. After all three selections are made, the character is named, a confirmation and selfies are received, and the process ends.
