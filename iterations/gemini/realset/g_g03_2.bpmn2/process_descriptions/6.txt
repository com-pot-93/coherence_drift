The process starts. A decision is made. If the answer is "Yes", account information is entered, a confirmation email is received, and a link in the email is clicked. If the answer is "No", the process skips directly to logging into Battle.net. After clicking the link in the email or choosing to log into Battle.net directly, account information is loaded. Another decision is made. If the answer is "Yes", a payment method is selected. Depending on the payment method chosen, either credit card information or IBAN/BIC information is entered. After entering either the credit card or IBAN/BIC information, or if the answer to the previous decision was "No", the game is logged into. Then, in parallel, the realm, race, and class are selected. After all three selections are made, the character is named, a confirmation and selfies are received, and the process ends.
