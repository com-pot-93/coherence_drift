The process starts. Then, a decision is made. If the answer is "Yes", account information is entered, a confirmation email is received, and a link in the email is clicked. Otherwise, if the answer is "No", the process skips directly to logging into Battle.net. After clicking the link in the email, the process proceeds to logging into Battle.net. After logging in, account information is loaded, and another decision is made. If the answer is "Yes", a payment method is selected. Then, depending on whether the payment method is "Credit Card" or "IBAN/BIC", the corresponding information is entered. After entering either the credit card or IBAN/BIC information, or if the answer to the previous decision is "No", the process continues with logging into the game. After logging in, in-game data and position are loaded. Subsequently, the process splits into parallel activities: selecting a realm, selecting a race, and selecting a class. Once all three selections are made, the process merges back together, and the character is named. Finally, a confirmation and selfies are received, and the process ends.
