The process starts. Then, a decision is made. If the answer is "Yes", account information is entered, a confirmation email is received, and a link in the email is clicked. If the answer is "No", the process skips directly to logging into Battle.net. After clicking the link in the email, the process proceeds to logging into Battle.net. After logging in, account information is loaded, and another decision is made. If the answer is "Yes", a payment method is selected, leading to another decision. If "Credit Card" is selected, credit card information is entered. If "IBAN/BIC" is selected, IBAN and BIC information is entered. After entering either the credit card or IBAN/BIC information, the process merges back. If the answer to the previous decision is "No", the process skips the payment method selection. After the merge, the game is logged into, and in-game data and position are loaded. Following this, three tasks occur in parallel: selecting a realm, selecting a race, and selecting a class. Once all three are completed, the character is named, a confirmation and selfies are received, and the process ends.
