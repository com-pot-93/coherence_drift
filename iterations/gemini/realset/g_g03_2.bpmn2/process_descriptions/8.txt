The process starts. Then, a decision is made. If the answer is "Yes," account information is entered, followed by receiving a confirmation email. If the answer is "No," the system is logged into. After either of these paths, account information is loaded. Another decision is made. If the answer is "Yes," a payment method is selected. Depending on the payment method chosen, either credit card information or IBAN/BIC information is entered. After entering either of these, the process continues. If the answer to the previous decision is "No," the process skips the payment method selection and information entry. After either of these paths, the game is logged into. Then, the process splits into three parallel activities: selecting a realm, selecting a race, and selecting a class. Once all three activities are completed, a character is named, followed by receiving a confirmation and selfies. The process ends.
