The process starts. Then, a decision is made. If the answer is "Yes", account information is entered, a confirmation email is received, and a link in the email is clicked. Otherwise, if the answer is "No", the system is logged into. After either of these paths, account information is loaded.

Next, another decision is made. If the answer is "Yes", a payment method is selected. Then, depending on the payment method, either credit card information or IBAN/BIC information is entered. After entering either of these, the process continues. If the answer to the previous decision is "No", the process skips directly to the next step.

After this, the game is logged into. Then, in parallel, a realm, a race, and a class are selected. Once all three selections are made, the character is named, a confirmation and selfies are received, and the process ends.
