The process starts. Then, two activities, "Confirm Job Application" and "Rate Job Application," are performed in parallel. After both are completed, a decision is made. If the answer is "Yes," the process proceeds to another decision. If the answer is "No," the "Negotiate Interview" and "Rate Job Seeker" activities are performed, after which the process proceeds to the mentioned decision. Depending on the outcome of this decision, either the "Hire Prospective Customer" activity is performed, or it is skipped. Finally, the process ends.
