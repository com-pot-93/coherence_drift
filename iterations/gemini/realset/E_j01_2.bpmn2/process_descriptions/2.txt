The process starts. Then, two activities occur in parallel: "Confirm Job Application" and "Rate Job Application". After both activities are completed, a decision is made. If a condition is met, the process proceeds directly to another decision. If the condition is not met, the "Negotiate Interview" and "Rate Job Seeker" activities are performed in sequence, before proceeding to the mentioned decision. Depending on the outcome of this decision, either the "Hire Prospective Customer" activity is performed, or the process proceeds directly. Finally, the process ends.
