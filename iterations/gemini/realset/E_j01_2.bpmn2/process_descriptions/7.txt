The process starts. Then, two activities occur in parallel: "Confirm Job Application" and "Rate Job Application". After both are completed, a decision is made. If the answer is "Yes", the process proceeds directly to another decision point. If the answer is "No", the "Negotiate Interview" and "Rate Job Seeker" activities are performed in sequence, before reaching the second decision point. Finally, the "Hire Prospective Customer" activity is performed, and the process ends.
