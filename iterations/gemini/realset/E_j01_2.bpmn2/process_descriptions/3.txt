The process starts. Then, two activities occur in parallel: "Confirm Job Application" and "Rate Job Application". After both activities are completed, a decision is made. If the condition is met, the process proceeds directly to another decision. If the condition is not met, the "Negotiate Interview" and "Rate Job Seeker" activities are performed, and then the process proceeds to the mentioned decision. Depending on whether the decision is "Yes" or "No", the process either performs the "Hire Prospective Customer" activity or proceeds directly to the end. Finally, the process ends.
