The process starts when a job application is received. Subsequently, the process splits into two parallel activities: confirming the job application and rating the job application. After both activities are completed, the process continues to a decision point. If a condition is met, the process proceeds directly to another decision point; otherwise, an interview is negotiated, and the job seeker is rated. Following this, the process reaches another decision point that either leads to hiring the prospective customer or directly to a final decision point. Finally, the process arrives at the mentioned decision point, and then it ends.
