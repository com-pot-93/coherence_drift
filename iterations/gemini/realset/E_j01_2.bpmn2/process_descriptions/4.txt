The process starts. Then, two activities occur in parallel: "Confirm Job Application" and "Rate Job Application". After both are completed, a decision is made. If the answer is "Yes", the process proceeds directly to another decision point. If the answer is "No", the "Negotiate Interview" task is performed, followed by the "Rate Job Seeker" task, and then the process proceeds to the mentioned decision point. Depending on the outcome of this decision, either the "Hire Prospective Customer" task is performed or the process proceeds directly to the next step. Finally, the process ends.
