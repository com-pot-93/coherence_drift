The process starts. Then, two activities occur in parallel: "Confirm Job Application" and "Rate Job Application". After both are completed, the process proceeds to a decision point. If the decision is "Yes", the process continues to another decision point. If the decision is "No", the process moves to "Negotiate Interview", followed by "Rate Job Seeker", and then proceeds to the mentioned decision point. Finally, the process executes "Hire Prospective Customer" and ends.
