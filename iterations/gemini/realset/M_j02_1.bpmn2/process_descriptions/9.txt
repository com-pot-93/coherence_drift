The process starts when a customized airplane order is received. The order is then processed. Following this, two activities occur in parallel: building the airplane base model and transmitting customization requirements. Once both are completed, a decision is made based on a condition. If the condition is true, the process waits for 14 days before proceeding. If the condition is false, the process proceeds immediately. Next, another decision is made based on whether a certain condition is met. If it is met, a reminder is sent. Otherwise, the process continues without sending a reminder. After this, bits and pieces are installed, followed by controlling engines and risk factors, and preparing for a test flight. The test flight protocol is then received and forwarded. Subsequently, a decision is made between standard and premium delivery. Depending on the choice, the airplane is shipped via standard or premium delivery. Finally, a confirmation email is received, and the process ends.
