The process starts when a customized airplane order is received. The order is then processed. Following this, two activities occur in parallel: building the airplane base model and transmitting customization requirements. Once both are completed, a decision is made based on a condition. If the condition is true, the process waits for 14 days before proceeding. If the condition is false, the process skips the waiting period. Next, another decision is made based on whether a certain condition is met. If it is, a reminder is sent; otherwise, the reminder is skipped. After this, bits and pieces are installed, followed by controlling engines and risk factors, and preparing for a test flight. The test flight protocol is then received and forwarded. A decision is then made between shipping via standard delivery or premium delivery. Finally, a confirmation email is received, and the process ends.
